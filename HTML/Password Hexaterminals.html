On the Subject of Password Hexaterminals

Those are some actual factual lexical hexical germinal terminals.

  1. The module consists of a hexagonal grid of terminals, each with a letter on each edge.
  2. Selecting a terminal rotates it 60° anticlockwise and Caesar shifts each of its letters one space forwards through the alphabet until it is rotated 360°, shifting the letters back to what was originally displayed.
  3. If a word is written across a straight line of letters across the grid, arrows will begin flickering denoting the writing direction of the word.
  4. The module is disarmed once a word is written across every straight line spanning the whole grid.
