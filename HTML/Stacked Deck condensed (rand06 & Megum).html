On the Subject of Conveniently Stacked Deck

I am the jack of all cards or something like that...

For those who are familiar with the original manual.

  • Lit BOB and 3+ batteries -> submit starting card.
  • Lit FRK and parallel port -> New Deck Order.
  • Target Card is a Spade and 3+ port plates -> Charles Gauci stack.
  • Serial port and SN contains S/R/L/P/T -> Mnemonica stack.
  • Last SN digit is prime -> Si Stebbins stack.
  • Starting Card suit = Target Card suit -> Bart Harding stack.
  • Vowel in SN -> Eight Threatening Kings stack.
  • 4 digits in SN -> add them up, deal that many cards and press Submit.
  • Otherwise -> deal exactly 11 cards and press Submit.
