On the Subject of Bronco Trolleys

One can only imagine how these must taste, especially with the orange peel…

A bronco trolley is Homestar Runner’s favorite after-school snack. It is made by spreading peanut butter on a Triscuit, and topping it with a slice of orange. Help him make as many bronco trolleys as possible by clicking on the Triscuit, the peanut butter, and then the orange. Work fast; you’ve only got 45 seconds. Up to 13 finished bronco trolleys will appear on the right side as you make them.

At least 10 bronco trolleys should be made by default, but the number needed can be changed in the mod’s settings to anywhere from 10 to 15.
