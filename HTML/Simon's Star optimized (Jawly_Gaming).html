Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Simon’s Star

On the Subject of Simon’s Sparkling Star

Twinkle twinkle little Simon's Star.

  • For use by those familiar with the original manual.
  • In the table, a ‘+’ denotes a clockwise movement. A ‘-’ denotes a counterclockwise movement. A ‘#’ symbol denotes the displayed number.
  • Note: stage 1 flash/stage 1 press: the color flashed/you pressed at stage 1.
Stage 1
  • If Red flashed, press Green + 2.
  • Else, if Blue flashed, press Yellow - #.
  • Else, if Yellow flashed, press Purple + #.
  • Else, if Green flashed, press Red - 1.
  • Else, press Blue - 2.
Stage 2
  • If Green flashed and stage 1 flash was Green, Yellow or Blue, press Blue - #.
  • Else, if Red flashed and stage 1 press was Yellow, Red or Purple, press Yellow + 3.
  • Else, if Blue flashed and stage 1 press was Purple or Yellow, press Green.
  • Else, if Yellow flashed and stage 1 flash was Green, Yellow, Blue or Purple, press Red - 2.
  • Else, if Purple flashed and stage 1 flash was Green or Red, press Purple + #.
  • Else, press stage 1 flash.

Stage 3
  • If three different colours have flashed, press Yellow − #.
  • Else, if two different colours have been pressed, press Blue + 2.
  • Else, if neither Green or Purple have been pressed, press Red + #.
  • Else, if Blue or Red flashed, press Purple − 1.
  • Else, press stage 1 press.
Stage 4
  • If stage 3 flash + # has been pressed, press stage 2 press
  • Else, if stage 4 flash  − 2 has NOT been pressed, press stage 3 flash − #.
  • Else, if stage 2 press  − # has flashed, press stage 1 flash.
  • Else, if stage 1 press + 2 has NOT been pressed, press stage 3 press + #.
  • Else, press stage 2 flash
Stage 5
  • If all five colours have flashed, press Green.
  • Else, if Purple has NOT been pressed, press Red.
  • Else, if Yellow has NOT flashed, press Blue.
  • Else, if Red − # has NOT been pressed, press Purple.
  • Else, if Blue + # has NOT flashed, press Yellow.
  • Else, if Green has both flashed and been pressed, press Red + #.
  • Else, press Blue − #.
