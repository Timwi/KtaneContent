This module contains a 5×5 matrix of cards of various types. This matrix will be any of the grids in the table on page 3, and it may be rotated. The word on the module will cycle through each card in reading order. Each card can contain any of the words on the table below. If a card is beige or black in its grid, it will appear on the module as black. If it is red or blue, it will appear on the module as pink.

You will be on either the red team or the blue team, based on the color of the module. Identify which grid of cards is present, and consult the table on page 3 to get a category of words. Submit any cards where its color in the grid is the same as your team’s color, and the category obtained from the table on page 3 contains its word. There may be more than one card to submit. Press the word to submit the current card.
