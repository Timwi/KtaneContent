Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Mislocation

On the Subject of Mislocation

You’ve never been this lost in an area this tiny.

This module displays six pressable vacuum tube lights. Four of which are in a square formation, one diagonally in the center and one in the bottom left. The four tubes in a square each refer to their respective direction. The center tube submits the current position and the tube in the bottom left relocates you to a random position.

The maze is a random shape with 19 square tiles, of which all but one are linked in pairs. Moving onto a tile will teleport you to the tile it’s paired with.

To solve the module, locate the unlinked tile.

There are no walls, however a tube will light up if there is no tile in its direction and the module will not allow you to move there.

Note that these boundaries are your only point of reference.
