Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Emotiguy Identification

On the Subject of Emotiguy Identification

Basic emotiguy learning material. Learn who to eat.

  • The module consists of a keyboard, a text box, a display with an image of with a Shutterstock logo, 3 unlit light bulbs, and a logo of Daz3D’s “Emotiguy”.
  • To initiate a stage, press the enter key on the keyboard. The logo will then reveal an Emotiguy.
  • Type the exact name of the Emotiguy shown in the module. Submit your answer by pressing the Enter button on the keyboard.
  • If the input given was correct, a light bulb beside the border will light up. Light up all the light bulbs given to solve the module.
