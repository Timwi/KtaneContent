Have you ever played a module that’s been out for over 5 years and still screw it up?

Highlight in the small grids below to represent raised dots on the module.
The small numbers are referred to as “positions”, and the large numbers are referred to as “Braille cell numbers”.

Below, start at hypothetical position 0. If you go past position 24, go back to position 1. For each serial number character, move forward X positions, where X is the corresponding character (converted to A1Z26 if it’s a letter) plus 1. Toggle the state of dot in the position you are now at (raised becomes unraised and vice versa) before repeating the procedure with subsequent characters.
