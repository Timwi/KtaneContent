Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Prankster

On the Subject of Prankster

Arguably fun.

This needy module will sound an alarm when a Prankster Comet arrives on your bomb. Each color of comet has a different effect.

Daredevil Comets (White)

While a daredevil comet is on your bomb, any strikes will immediately detonate your bomb.

Cosmic Comets (Purple)

While a cosmic comet is on your bomb, a shadow will follow your cursor across the screen. Allowing your cursor to come into contact with the shadow will incur a strike.

Purple Comets (Pink)

When a Purple comet arrives on your bomb, up to three modules will gain a pink border. Highlight all of them before the comet leaves, or else you will receive a strike.
