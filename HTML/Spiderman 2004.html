On the Subject of Spiderman 2004s

Spider-Man 2 is a 2004 action-adventure video game, based on the film of the same name. Published by Activision, the console versions were developed by Treyarch, while the others had different developers and are drastically different as a result. The game is the sequel to Spider-Man (2002), itself based on the film of the same name. The Treyarch-developed versions are considered a landmark title in the industry for being the first superhero video game to incorporate a full open world design.

  • Spiderman 2004 is a module in where the Spiderman 2 game from 2004 has taken over the background. To disarm Spiderman 2004, press Spiderman 2004’s button 2004 times, which happens to be the year that Spiderman 2004 came out.

Appendix Spiderman 2004: Identifying Modules That May Look Like Spiderman 2004

Worry not, there are actually small differences in each mod that differentiate them from being Spiderman 2004.

  • If there is a piano on the module, you are looking at Piano Keys, NOT Spiderman 2004.
  • If there is a tesseract on the module, you are looking at The Hypercube, NOT Spiderman 2004.
  • If there is a Kanye West, you are looking at Trick Or Treat, NOT Spiderman 2004.
  • If there is a christmas tree on the module, you are looking at Christmas Presents, NOT Spiderman 2004.
  • If there is a Goofy’s Game on the module, you are looking at Goofy’s Game, NOT Spiderman 2004.
  • If there is a Annoying Orange on the module, you are looking at Vtuber Identification, NOT Spiderman 2004.
  • If there is a text that says SNACK ATTACK, you are looking at Bakery, NOT Spiderman 2004.
  • If there is a ??? on the module, you are looking at Variety, NOT Spiderman 2004.
  • If there is a Spiderman 2004 on the module, you are looking at Spiderman 2004, NOT Spiderman 2004.
  • If there is a British chainsmoker on the module, you are looking at Bottom Gear, NOT Spiderman 2004.
  • If there is a Allison Eckhart on the module, you are looking at Allison Eckhart, NOT Allison Eckhart.
