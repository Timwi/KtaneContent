On the Subject of Soloing Binary LEDs

I'm not afraid of the RED wire, it's just that we don't need to cut it.

These three codes this manual will refer to as keys.
Note each key is given a color.

Which keys are present uniquely define the sequence and therefore the answer.
Combine present keys via mixing additively to find which wire to cut and when.

W K
C R
M G
B Y
  • Cut GREEN when Grayscale. otherwise cut BLUE.
  • Note that the pairs below are opposites.
    • 12 and start or end ⇒ 123 125
    • CaR 23
    • MuG last 4 or 1 ⇒ 2345 5
    • BuY 1 T-O or T-W-O 5 ⇒ 12345 125
