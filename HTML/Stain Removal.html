On the Subject of Stain Removal

With our range of products, you can clean any mess. Wash it off! Dry it off! Jerk it off! We don’t care what you do with them or where the stains even came from! Silks! Suedes! Swag! Remember, the fluids you find are yours to do with as you please. Now get out of my house!

  • On the module is a piece of stained fabric forming of an 8×8 grid of squares and a selection of five detergents.
  • Each detergent removes a stain from the squares within its corresponding 5×5 subgrid depending on whether or not that square’s condition is met.
  • Some stains are more stubborn than others, requiring multiple treatments.
  • Completely remove the stain by selecting the appropriate detergents and the center squares of their subgrids on the fabric.
    Each detergent can be used three times at most.
  • Attempting to remove a stain from an already clean square will bleach the fabric, resulting in a strike and requiring the module to be reset.
