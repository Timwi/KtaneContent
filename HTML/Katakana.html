On the Subject of Katakana

プリーズ ベストウ ミー ウィズ サム グッド フレイバー テキスト、 オー グレート ワン~

Underneath the veil of a dark module, a vibrant green Japanese symbol surfaces. To those unfamiliar, its intense stare cuts through the abyss.

Using your keyboard, enter the corresponding romaji (the English transcription of Japanese) for each displayed character to solve the module.
There are 5 stages in total. A strike will occur upon an incorrect submission and the displayed character(s) will change, but the current stage number will not change.
