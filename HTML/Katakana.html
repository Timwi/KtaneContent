On the Subject of Katakana

プリーズ ベストウ ミー ウィズ サム グッド フレイバー テキスト、 オー グレート ワン~

Underneath the veil of a dark module, a vibrant green Japanese symbol surfaces. To those unfamiliar, its intense stare cuts through the abyss. Temporary bug: drop the bomb before selecting a different module to unbind the keyboard capture.

Using your keyboard, enter the corresponding romaji (the English transcription of Japanese) for each displayed character to solve the module.

There are 5 stages in total. A strike will incur upon an incorrect combination. Stages do not reset.
