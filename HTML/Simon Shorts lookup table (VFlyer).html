On the Subject of Disambiguating Simon Shorts

Why would he make confusing diagrams and still call THAT thrilling!?

  • This module has four colored buttons arranged in a diamond shape and a wire connecting every pair of buttons. A spark will occasionally travel between two buttons.
  • To solve the module, press the correct button determined from where the spark is being sent from and sending to for four stages.
  • For each stage examine the direction of the button where the spark is traveling from and to to get an action.

  • U, D, R: Press the button in that position, where U, D, and R are up, down, and right respectively.
  • CF: Using the diagram provided, press the button on the module whose position on the diagram corresponds to the color of the button sending the spark.
  • CT: Using the diagram provided, press the button on the module whose position on the diagram corresponds to the color of the button receiving the spark.
