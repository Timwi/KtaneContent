On the Subject of Assembly Code

Why are registers important, when you are already modifying them!

On the module, there are 3 displays. The left display will have 8 "codes", the top display will have a description of what a "code" does, and the right display tells you the position of the list your in.

You can also use the 2 buttons to cycle through the descriptions.

To solve the module, multiply the number of AA batteries by 2 and add 1. Modulo 8 and add 1 again. This number is the "code" your going to solve for in each stage.
