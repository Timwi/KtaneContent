Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Special Flowers

On the Subject of Special Flowers

Guide on how to get first place in BBT2025:
Step 1:

  • This module is initially empty. Selecting it for the first time will make it play a sequence of 20 flowers appearing to the rhythm of the song that plays along, with their appearances split into 4 groups of 5. Your goal is to memorise this sequence.
  • Inputting the letter ‘P’ using your keyboard will make the module replay the sequence whilst also accepting input:
    • Hovering over (or very near) a flower within 0.2 seconds of it appearing will make it turn green. Failing to do so will make it turn red.
    • If, by the end of the sequence, all 20 flowers turned green, the module will solve. The module will not strike for failing to hover over a flower.
  • The 20 flowers will always appear in the spots shown on the diagram below.
  • Inputting the letter ‘L’ using your keyboard while the sequence is not playing will make the flowers show their labels for a few seconds. Their labels will match the ones shown on the diagram below.
