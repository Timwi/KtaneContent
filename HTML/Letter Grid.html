On the Subject of Letter Grid

Guess you should've taken a foreign language in high school, huh?

  • The module has one of every letter from A-Z excluding J in a 5x5 grid. However, each letter will be encrypted differently.
  • The grid will contain one or more (albeit very rarely) words from a word list below.
  • Select the row or column that has a word from the word list, spelled either forwards or backwards.
