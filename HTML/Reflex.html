Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Reflex

On the Subject of Reflex

Weird reflex but ok.

This module features two rows of LEDs and a red button. To disarm this module you must light up all LEDs in the top row.

The module will highlight a LED in white in the top row. The bottom row will cycle having one LED lit at a time.

Press the button when the top LED and the corresponding LED in the bottom row are both lit. Repeat this until all LEDs are lit.

Pressing the button when the wrong LED is lit will cause a strike. The speed of the bottom row cycling will speed up with every LED lit.
