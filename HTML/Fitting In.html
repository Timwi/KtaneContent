On the Subject of Fitting In

Entering Bosnian mode.

A Fitting In module will initially show a hexomino.

Press the 'FLIP!' button to flip over the board. Doing so will reveal a 10 by 10 grid of black and white squares. You may freely press the 'FLIP!' button.

The hexomino will fit into the white squares of the grid, albeit the hexomino may be rotated/reflected. The hexomino will only appear once.

Pressing the six squares that the hexomino fits in will solve the module. Pressing an incorrect square will incur a strike.
