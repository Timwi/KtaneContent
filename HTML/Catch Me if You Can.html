When the display is pressed, a car will speed along the road, then six similar numberplates will be presented. For three rounds, select the numberplate that was on the car you just saw.

In Rounds 1 & 2, the car will drive towards the camera. In Round 3, it will drive away from it.

Selecting an incorrect answer will cause a strike and reset the current round.
