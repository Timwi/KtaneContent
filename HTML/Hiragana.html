On the Subject of Hiragana

はじまりのいっぽ、ゆめのみちへ.

In the shadow of a jet-black module, a bold green Japanese character emerges. For the uninitiated, its defiant gaze pierces from the void. Temporary bug: drop the bomb before selecting a different module to unbind the keyboard capture.

Using your keyboard, enter the corresponding romaji (the English transcription of Japanese) for each displayed character to solve the module.

There are 5 stages in total. A strike will incur upon an incorrect combination. Stages do not reset.
