Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Westeros

On the Subject of Westeros

An explosion is coming...

  • The module shows four cyclable text displays, a cyclable sigil display and an armilla submit button.
  • Set the displays so that they show the sigil, forename, house name, words, and ancestral seat of the same Westerosi house. Cycle the displays by clicking them.
  • Press the armilla button when they are set correctly to disarm the module.
  • Pressing the armilla button with incorrect information will cause a strike and reset the module.
