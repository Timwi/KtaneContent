On the Subject of USA Cycles

“Your whole miserable country is the size of one state!”

  • The mod will feature a set of cycling states from the United States colored either red or blue. Each state will be colored based on the electoral votes in the 2020 election as seen below.
  • The left button will speed up the cycling states, the right button will toggle their cycling, and the middle one will enter submission mode.
  • Each state has a two-letter abbreviation also found on the map. One of the two letters will be a “fake” letter, and you should ignore it.
  • All of the “real” letters will spell a word from the Declaration of Independence, the first letter of this word is the state that is underlined on the module. Once you have obtained this word, go into submission mode and type in that word using your keyboard.
  • Pressing the red button at this state will delete your word. Pressing blue will revert the mod back to its original state. Pressing the white button will submit your current word. The module accepts all valid solutions.
