On the Subject of Lights On

Commence confused random mashing of buttons!

On this module is a hexagonal grid consisting of 24 triangular tiles.

When the module is active, these tiles will turn either green or red, indicating whether or not it should be switched on when the timer expires.

Selecting a tile will toggle itself and any tile that shares an edge with the selected tile.
