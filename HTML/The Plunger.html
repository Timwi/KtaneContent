On the Subject of The Plunger

Houston, we have an easy needy module.

  • On the module, you will see a colored plunger with text on it.
  • Using the tables below, look up the two numbers using the plunger’s color and text, and how many times it has activated before.
  • If the needy has activated more than five times, restart at one.
  • Add the two digits received from the table, and if it is over 10, subtract 10 until it is a single digit.
  • Press the plunger when the last seconds digit of the bomb timer is equal to the digit you have calculated.
