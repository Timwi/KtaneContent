On the Subject of Name Changer

The word and drow are the same!

This module has 4 arrows, a submit button with the letter X, and the initial word generated from the list below.

Pressing the down or up button will move your word forwards or backwards in reading order through the list.

Only one letter of the word will be displayed. Pressing the left and right arrows will change the position of the letter displayed.


To solve the module, submit the correct letter of the correct word.

  1. To find the correct word, first find the initial word in the below table and take its position in reading order.
  2. Add this position to the number of ports. Subtract 24 if this sum is greater than 24. The correct word is the word in this position in reading order.
  3. To find the correct letter to submit, take the position received from step 1 and add the number of indicators. Modulo this by 10, then add 1. You will be submitting the letter in this position of the correct word.
