On the Subject of Name Changer

The word, and drow, are the same!

This module has 4 arrows, a submit button with the letter X, and the initial word generated from the list below. To get the position of a letter, you need to do the following:

  • Find the position of the initial word in the list below (in reading order).
  • Add the position of the initial word to the number of indicators.

Now use the number you got modulo 10 (if 0, then add 1) as the position of the another word to be found.

To find this such word, use:

  • The position of the initial word again.
  • Add the position of the initial word to the number of ports.

Modulo 24 after the steps. After you find the word, use the up and down buttons to find the word found, and the left and right buttons to find the letter found.
