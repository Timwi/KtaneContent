On the Subject of Netherite

Works only on ruleseed 1 and with no other solved Netherites.

Search for your SN digits, prepending underscores until it is four characters long and appending a colon.

If there is a Voltage Meter:

  • If the voltage is not a whole number, disregard columns 1 and 2. Otherwise, disregard 3 and 4.
  • If the voltage is greater than 5, disregard columns 1 and 3. Otherwise, disregard 2 and 4.

Otherwise:

  • If the SN or the sum of SN digits contains a number matching the number of lit indicators, disregard columns 1 and 2. Otherwise, disregard columns 3 and 4.
  • If the SN or the sum of SN digits contains a number matching the number of unlit indicators, disregard columns 1 and 3. Otherwise, disregard columns 2 and 4.
