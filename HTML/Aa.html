On the Subject of aa

aaaaaaaaa

This needy module will have a display and a number pad.

When the needy activates, you have 30 seconds to convert the value displayed to scientific notation.

Type in the conversion from aa to scientific notation, and enter the exponent. ADD ALL LEADING DIGITS! (12=012)
