On the Subject of Needy Hotate

I’m die, thank you forever.

There are nine buttons on the module, each with a different label. The labels may change every activation. Press three of them in the correct order to deactivate it.

If the number of activations is odd (1st time, 3rd time, 5th time...), press "HO", "TA", then "TE".

If the number of activations is even (2nd time, 4th time, 6th time...), press "TE", "TA", then "HO".
