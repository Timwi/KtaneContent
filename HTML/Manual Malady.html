Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Manual Malady

On the Subject of Manual Malady

M’lady, my manual, a merry melody in a malady medley.

The module presents an array of eight rainbow-coloured buttons, a green play button and a red submit button below.

Each coloured button represents an audio clip. Pressing the play button would play each audio clip from left to right without delay. The colour of the screen text corresponds to the section currently played.

These audio clips, when arranged in the correct order, play out the description of a random module.

This module in question would also show its name on the screen briefly when the bomb is activated before glitching out.

To solve the module, sort the sections in the correct order, then press submit.

Press two coloured buttons above to swap them. Pressing the play button whilst the module is playing its audio will stop it.

A strike will be given for an incorrect arrangement but will not reset the module. The name of the chosen module would also display itself on the screen briefly before glitching out once again.
