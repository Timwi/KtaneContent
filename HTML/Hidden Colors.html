On the Subject of Hidden Colors

Each button on the module represents a color. While the right LED is unlit, pressing a button will show its color on the display.

Pressing the green button will switch on the LED, allowing you to press a button to solve the module. Pressing an incorrect button will cause a strike and switch off the LED.

Be sure to keep note of the button colors before the LED is switched on!

Pressing a button will highlight its number on the side display. The buttons are ordered from 1 to 20 starting from the top-left and going in reading order.

The color of the LED determines which buttons are named "A", "B", "C", "D", "E" and "F". Use the following table to find the correct buttons to use:
