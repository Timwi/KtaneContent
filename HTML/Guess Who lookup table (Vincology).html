Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Guess Who?

On the Subject of Guess Whomst?

Round the outside, round the outside, round the outside...

The module consists of a rectangular display on the left, a button below the rectangular display, and 5 displays on the right.

Your job is to use the information that will be given on the left display to figure out the name of the Guess Who character. The names of the Guess Who characters are all exactly 5 letters in length. If an incorrect name is guessed, a strike will be recorded and the module will reset.

You can initiate the module by pressing the Recall button. The display will then show the answers to 8 Guess Who questions which can only be answered with “YES” or “NO.” Use the color of each response to determine which question is being asked. The answers may not appear in the order given below.

Color: Question:
Red Is your character female?
Orange Is your character smiling?
Yellow Does your character have light skin?
Green Does your character have glasses?
Blue Does your character have green eyes?
Violet Does your character have black hair?
Cyan Does your character wear a hat?
Pink Does your character have attached earlobes?

Use the eight answers in the table below to locate the correct cell to input into the five displays on the right. A colored header indicates a YES answer, and a prepended "!" as well as a lack of color indicates a NO answer.
