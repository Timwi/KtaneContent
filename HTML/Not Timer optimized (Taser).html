Perform the first action that applies.

  • 0 strikes and lit CAR, Hold timer.
  • 0 strikes and batteries ≥ 2, Tap strike.
  • Time ≤ 30 minutes, Tap timer.
  • Batteries ≥ 2, Hold timer.
  • Parallel, Tap strike.
  • 2 strikes and lit FRK, Tap timer.
  • Unlit SND, Tap timer.
  • Even in serial, Tap strike.
  • Serial and lit indicator > 1, Hold timer.
  • 0 indicators, Tap timer.
  • lit indicators ≥ 2,Hold strike.
  • 0 batteries, Tap strike.
  • Vowel in serial, Tap timer.
  • Port plates > 2, Hold timer.
  • Hold timer.

Releasing a Held Strike Indicator

  • 0 strikes: Hold for 3 seconds, release on 2.
  • 1 strike: Hold for 6 seconds, release on 3.
  • 2 strikes: Hold for 9 seconds, release on 9.
  • 3+ strikes: Hold for 12 seconds, release on 8.

Releasing a Held Timer

The rules refer to the time you held the button.

  • 1 minute: Hold for 1 second, release on 1.
  • 30 minutes:Hold for 3 seconds, release on 4.
  • 60 minutes: Hold for 6 seconds, release on 7.
  • 120 minutes: Hold for 9 seconds, release on 6.
  • 180 minutes: Hold for 12 seconds, release on 5.
  • Else: Hold for 14 seconds, release on 3.
