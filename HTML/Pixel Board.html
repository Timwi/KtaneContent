Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Pattern Plane

On the Subject of Pixel Boards

What is a pixel but a miserable little pile of color?

This module has a six-by-six grid of individually pressable pixels. To begin, press any one. This will cause a series of seven directions to play.

There is only one place in the nine original mazesmazes where this series of directions fits. Press the end pixel.

Then, two more directions will be shown. Move between mazes in those directions, wrapping around if necessary. Then, navigate that maze to the marked exit to solve the module.

Upon striking, all shown directions will repeat.
