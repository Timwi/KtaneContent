On the Subject of The Stampycat

Meow

Pet the cat to add 2 seconds to the timer.

The needy will deactivate when the counter reaches 100.

Petting the cat while it’s inactive, or while the timer is below 10 seconds will cause a strike.

If the needy strikes, it will also deactivate.
