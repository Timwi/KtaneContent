On the Subject of Metalmaking

...You’re telling me I need *Gravitase* soil to fuse Time?!

You have 8 meteos, and a goal - make a rare meteo.

To make a rare meteo, you need to incinerate 5 different meteos to forge it.

Each rare meteo has different needs for the incinerated meteos to correctly forge into it.

  • Soul meteos require a big and hot combination, while being of low mass.
  • Time meteos require a high mass and big combination, while being cooler.
  • Chi meteos require a hot and high mass combination, while being small.

Each of the 8 meteos on the module has these three features - Heat, Mass, and Volume.

The next two tables' values, when multiplied together, will give each meteo these three values.

Find a set of 5 meteos where each sequential value for the rare metal, as ordered above, is at least 8 below the last after all 5 are summed.

IE: A Soul with 36 Volume, 24 Heat, and 12 Mass

After you have found the 5 meteos you plan to forge with, you must now find the order in which you must forge them.

The first meteo to be forged must heat the forge to be hot enough to use. The hottest meteo you will use will do.

Secondly, add the most massive meteo, then thirdly, the most volume.

Fourth, forge the first clockwise of the remaining two after the first meteo you forged, then finally forge the last remaining one.

If multiple meteos fit a condition, you may use either.
