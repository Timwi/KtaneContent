Main View

This is where you start out. You start with a Wooden Axe and 10 Cooked Beef.
Clicking on an item will consume 1 hunger to attempt to gather that item. You start out with 10 hunger. If your hunger reaches 0, you will receive a strike. Attempting to gather without the required tools will result in a strike.
Clicking on Cooked Beef will consume one to restore 4 hunger. Having no Cooked Beef when attempting to eat will result in nothing.
Clicking on the blocks at the top-left (Grass Block, Netherrack, End Stone) will attempt to enter their respective dimension. Entering a dimension for the first time will attempt to unlock it first. If you don’t have the required items to unlock, you will strike and no dimension change will occur.
Clicking on the chest will let you enter your inventory.

Inventory

Hovering over items will display how much of that item you have. You can have a maximum of 64 of each item at a time.
Clicking on certain items will attempt to craft the item you click. If you don’t have the required items to craft, you will get a strike.
When you have the Ender Dragon defeated, clicking on the Dragon Egg in the inventory will solve the module.
Clicking on the chest will exit you back to Main View.

Encounter

After you obtain a sword, an encounter could start after gathering. This will happen every 6-12 attempts to gather.
The top middle of the module displays mob HP and mob drop, while the bottom middle bar displays your HP. You start out with 10 HP.
Clicking on Cooked Beef will consume one to restore 3.5 HP to you. Having no Cooked Beef when attempting to eat will result in nothing.
Clicking on the sword will attack the current mob, reducing their HP by your sword’s DMG. After attacking, if the mob HP goes to 0 or below, you will receive the mob drop and return to Main View. Otherwise, the mob will have a 50%* chance to attack back, reducing your HP by its ATK minus your armor’s DEF. If your HP goes to 0 or below, you will strike and return to Main View.
After an encounter, your HP and hunger will be fully restored to 10.
