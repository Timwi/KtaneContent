On the Subject of Talking Points

F-fear of public speaking?

Navigate the slideshow using the two pointing buttons on the bottom of the module.

To solve the module, figure out exactly how many total points the slideshow is worth.

  • The worth of picture slides are detailed in the large table beginning on the next page.
  • Once you have figured out the total worth of all picture slides, follow the instructions under "Award Multipliers."
  • If a skeleton is visible on the top of the module, divide the final value by 2 and round down if necessary.

To submit, press the button in the bottom-left corner and enter your final point value.
