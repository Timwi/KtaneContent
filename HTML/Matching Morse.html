Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Matching Morse

On the Subject of Matching Morse

Get your dots on the spot and don’t clash your dashes!

The 9 LEDs on the module will each be flashing a Morse code character.

Every LED except one will be paired with another. Paired LEDs will flash the same character, although not necessarily simultaneously.

Press two paired LEDs in succession to match them. This will cause them to stop blinking.

After each LED pair is matched, the module will solve. Do not attempt to match the LED with the unique character.
