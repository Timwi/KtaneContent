Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Matching Morse

On the Subject of Matching Morse

Get your dots on the spot and don’t clash your dashes!

This module displays nine blinking LEDs in a 3×3 arrangement.

Each LED is transmitting a character in Morse code.

Each LED except one is paired with another LED on the module.

The two LEDs in each pair will be transmitting the same character, however they may not be synchronised.

Select any LED followed by its paired LED. If you are correct these LEDs will stop transmitting. If you are incorrect you will receive a strike.

Attempting to pair the unpaired LED with any LED will result in a strike.

Select all LED pairs to solve the module.
