On the Subject of Timing is Everything

1... 2... 3... go!

This module will display a time on the screen and you must press the submit button at that exact time. Once you press it, a new time will come up and you must once again press the submit button at that exact time. Pressing it too early or missing the time will result in you gaining a strike and starting over. Once you complete 3 stages, you will have solved the module.
