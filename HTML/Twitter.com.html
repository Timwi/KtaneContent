You’ve decided to log onto Twitter to make your daily post for the lovely Twitter clout. However, it seems like the other users are in a particularly bad mood today, and are ready to cancel people for even the most simple and unassuming posts! Undeterred, you decide to try and go ahead with making your post, though at an enormous risk.

To solve the module, you must decide which of the currently saved drafts you have stored away to post and when to post it.

Obtaining the Clout Value:

First, press the Twitter logo to log into the module and deduce whose profile you’re using based on the profile picture in the top left. Use this to find the Clout Formula associated with that user. Define the required variables and use the formula to obtain the value of C. Take the absolute value of C, drop any decimals, and then take the least significant digit. This is your Clout Value.

Note: For the variables that require it, modulo to a range of 0-4.

Tweeting the Right Tweet at the Right Time:

Once you have your Clout Value, find which set of Tweets below matches that Clout Value. Find which Tweet within the set is present in the drafts and post that Tweet at the corresponding Tweeting Time. If the correct Tweet is posted at the correct time, you will defuse the module. If you post the incorrect Tweet or at the wrong time, you will be canceled, you will receive a strike, and you will be given a new profile to Tweet from alongside new Tweet drafts.
