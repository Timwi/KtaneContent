  • Four symbols will be passed through the window of an x-ray scanner simultaneously, which shows a thin section of the symbols scanning either from top to bottom or bottom to top.
  • Each of the symbols will be taken from the below table, and will represent either the red, green, blue, or value (brightness) channel.
  • As the symbols pass through the scanner, the components from each of the four will be combined, and the result will be displayed on the scanner. Components will appear if the corresponding part of the symbol is shaded.
  • Each of the channels and their symbol corresponds to a number in the table below. To solve the module, press the number which doesn't appear.
