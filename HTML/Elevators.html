On the Subject of Elevators

Hold it for me!

The module consists of an elevator with a display showing the current floor of the elevator, and 2 buttons, one for going up and one for going down. You must bring the elevator to your floor, and then bring it to its destination floor by pressing a direction button, waiting until it reaches the correct floor, and then pressing it again to stop the elevator.

Finding your current floor

Using the starting floor of the elevator, look up your current floor in the table.

Finding your destination floor

When the elevator arrives at the correct floor, it will open up and reveal 1-2 people standing inside*. In addition to this, it may also play a sound effect. Based on the clothes of the people and the sound effect, use the following tables to figure out how many floors to travel. The direction is in the opposite direction you went at the start.

*If any person has a nameplate that says "Bob", ignore all other rules and go up to floor 606. Bob will stop the elevator for you.
