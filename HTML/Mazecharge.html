On the Subject of Mazecharging

I’m going to guess that this is meant to occupy your attention, because otherwise this is stolen off of a restaurant placemat.

  • Overload the capacitor by navigating the light, through one of mazes below, to the off spaces.
  • The walls of the initial maze correspond to the first character of the serial number.
  • Each off space allows charge to build for between 7 and 15 seconds before turning back on.
  • Once there are no off spaces remaining, the maze is regenerated, cycling to the next character of the serial number (or to the first from the last).
  • Warning: Do not cross the walls of the maze, which are invisible on the module.
    Doing so will discharge the module, resetting the off spaces of the current maze.
