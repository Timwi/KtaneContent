On the Subject of LOOK AT ME

I’M THE MODULE NOW

Whenever another module other than LOOK AT ME is selected, LOOK AT ME may appear instead.

Press the button that appears to satisfy LOOK AT ME’s needs.

If all other modules have been solved or LOOK AT ME has been satisfied enough times, LOOK AT ME will solve.
