On the Subject of Uncolored Keys

Who unpainted this keypad?

The module has four gray keys with letters and a display with the name of a color. If the keys are not gray, you are looking at a different module.

Press the key which has the highest value. This is the alphabetical position of the key’s letter multiplied by the key’s color value from the table below. If multiple keys are tied for the highest value, any of them may be pressed.

Gray Red Yellow Blue Green White
1 2 3 4 5 6

This module has 4 stages. The actual color of what is on the display indicates what stage the module is on:

  • White - Stage 1
  • Yellow - Stage 2
  • Blue - Stage 3
  • Purple - Stage 4

After each stage, the key pressed receives the name of the color on the display.
