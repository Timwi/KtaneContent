On the Subject of Jenga

How do you stack up?

Remove all possible pieces from the tower without toppling the tower by selecting an incorrect block or by having the tower be off balance.

Look at the symbols on the short faces of the tower’s pieces.

The lighter green areas of the chart mean that this piece is safe to pull.

The darker red areas of the chart mean that this piece is not safe to pull.
