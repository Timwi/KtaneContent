  1. Look at each button: there is one color and a label on the button. Purple represents both red and blue.
  2. For each button, use the table below to decide whether or not to press the button.
  3. Press the buttons in the order listed in the bottom table. If no buttons are pressed, press the second button in the list.
  4. Upon getting a strike, the entire module resets.
