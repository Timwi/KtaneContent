Decrypt Instructions

  • Concatenate the encrypted word with the letters on screen 2 in that order.
  • Find the Nth letter of the encrypted word in the Stunted Polybius Square and determine its row and column.
  • Then find the dot pattern in the Blind Polybius Square using the matching row and column numbers.
  • Stack the dot patterns on top of one another, then re-divide them into blocks of three rows each, and read the result as Unified English Braille to obtain the decrypted word.
