Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Literally Malding

On the Subject of Literally Malding

Silly me, I knew the yellow Illuminati was up to no good!

  • If Grunkle Squeaky is NOT present, you are looking at a different Needy Module.
  • This needy module features a wild Grunkle Squeaky and his fez.
  • In order to deactivate the needy, press the fez when Grunkle Squeaky has no fez.
  • Grunkle Squeaky bends time and space, so there is a barrier around him to prevent it from catastrophic disasters.
  • If you do not give the fez to Grunkle Squeaky, he will begin Malding (being Mad and Balding), and a strike will be given.
