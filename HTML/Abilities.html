Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Abilities

On the Subject of Abilities

"Imagine if I had a real weapon!" -Jax

  • The module exists of a screen and a selection box.
  • The screen will display an ability icon from League of Legends.
  • Determine which name matches with the ability that is shown.
  • Press the left and right arrow buttons to cycle between possible answers.
  • Press the centre button to submit the current ability name.
  • Submitting the wrong ability name will cause a strike and a new ability will be generated.
