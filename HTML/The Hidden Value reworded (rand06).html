On the Subject of Speedrunning HV

Come on, it could be done WAY faster!

  • Read all the digits and their colors, rotating the bomb. There are gonna be 4-6 colored digits.
  • Digits (call them N) are the number of the hinge. Hinges are numbered 1-8 in reading order.
  • Hinge with N = 0 is hinge number (2nd SN digit - 1).
  • Do all the 9's LAST. Use any remaining hinges.
  • Find intersection of N and color (call it X).
  • Press hinge N when the last digit of the seconds timer is X.
  • Repeat for all digits.
