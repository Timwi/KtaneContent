Appendix V0LT: Voltage Meter Identification Reference

A rectangular or half circle electric meter can be found on the sides of the bomb casing.

This meter will display the voltage of the circuit running through the bomb in Volts (V). Here are the properties the voltage will always follow:

  • Constant value throughout the bomb.
  • Either a whole number or halfway between 2 whole numbers.
  • At least 1V but no more than 10V.
