Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Crazy Talk With A K

On the Subject of Crazy Talk With A K

Text, when distorted properly, can only be read at certain angles. This module utilizes this fact.

  • Two skewed words will be displayed on the module. At any point if the number is above 100, subtract 100 until it is less than 100.
  • Take the alphanumerical sums of each of the words and multiply them against each other.
  • Multiply that against the last digit of the serial number, if zero then don’t do anything.
  • Multiply that against the number of batteries, if zero then don’t do anything.
  • Input that number and press the hovering sphere.
