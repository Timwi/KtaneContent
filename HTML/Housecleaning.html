On the Subject of Housecleaning

Who left this banana peel on the ground?!

On the module is a 5×5 section of a house. Locate this section on the layout below and clean the spaces with various products that you have at your disposal.

Trash will be invisible to the defuser’s eyes.

(Select the product before you select a square!)
