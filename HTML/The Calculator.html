On the Subject of The Calculator

For once the bomb makers decided to throw you a bone...

  • Take the sum of each character in the serial number, with letters being their alphanumerical position plus nine.
  • Input the number onto the calculator and then press solve.
  • Other features that The Calculator includes are:
    • Perform any basic arithmetic operation.
    • Square any number and take the square root of any number.
    • Take the digital root of any number.
    • Modulo any number.
  • Use the ANS button to finalize any operation, the C button to clear any input, and the D button to delete the last input.
  • Do note that the buttons =, X, Y, Z, and . are broken and do not do anything.
