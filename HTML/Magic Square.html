On the Subject of Magic Squares

But how do you add up to your fellow peers?

  • This module has a sliding scrambled magic square.
  • A magic square is a matrix of numbers with the property that each row and column share the same sums, as well as both diagonals.
  • To disarm the module, simply rearrange the square in such a way that it shows a valid magic square.
