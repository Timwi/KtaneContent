On the Subject of Icon Reveal

Who’s That Pokémon?

This module has a slightly covered-up module icon with a counter and a submit button.

Figure out what module on your bomb the icon represents and find its periodic table symbol from the Repository of Manual Pages' "Periodic Table" view. Cycle the given symbols to select the correct one.

Once you have used the arrows to enter your value, press the submit button.
