Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Subbly Jubbly

On the Subject of Subbly Jubbly

I wibble you the bitchy of ligma.

This module initially consists of a cycling display and and a button.

The display cycles through nine words each beginning with different letters.
Note down all nine words and press the button the rotate the board and begin the quiz.

The back of the board consists of a large screen and a keyboard.
When the quiz begins, a three minute timer will begin to tick down and a question about a modded module will be displayed on the screen.
The answer to this question can be found in the module's manual.

But wait, there’s more:

The first* time a word in the answer begins with the same letter as a word in the initially displayed cycle, replace that word with the displayed word.

Enter the answer into the module using the keyboard:

  • The lettered keys are in QWERTY format.
  • There are no symbols present on the keyboard:
    • & symbols are written out as the word “and”.
    • All other punctuation marks are removed from the answer.
  • The key to the left of the keyboard deletes the last character of the entry.
  • The key to the immediate right of the keyboard adds a space. Spaces can only proceed letters.
  • The key to the far right submits the entry.

If the entry is correct, the module will move on to the next question.
Answer all three questions correctly to solve the module.
Submitting an incorrect answer or running out of time will result in a strike and reset the module, rotating the board back around to display the words once again.

*The following words do not get replaced: the, a, an, and, au, aux, of, is.
Skip over them when applying word replacements.
