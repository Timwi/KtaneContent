On the Subject of Cursed Double-Oh

I swear it didn’t look like this last time I saw it.

  • The four arrow buttons have the following functions in a randomized order:
    • Move 3 spaces up or down (wrapping around the edges)
    • Move 3 spaces left or right (wrapping around the edges)
    • Move up or down (wrapping) within the current small grid
    • Move left or right (wrapping) within the current small grid
  • The module begins at a random location in the grid. Navigate to the 0 in the center and press the submit button (bottom-right) to disarm the module.
  • Do not navigate to a square already visited.
  • Press the submit button on any non-zero digit to reset the module. A new starting position is selected and the buttons’ movement functions are reshuffled.
