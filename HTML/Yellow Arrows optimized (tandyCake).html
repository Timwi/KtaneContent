On the Subject of Yellow Arrows

Pno Ofunny Irules Nare Tallowed Shere!

On the module are 4 directional buttons, and a display screen in the middle.

If the buttons are not Yellow, you’re looking at a different module.

Use the list according to the last digit of the serial number. Take the next 5 letters in the sequence in order, starting from the displayed letter and wrapping around. Do not use the rule of the displayed letter. If a number has multiple strings, stay within that string.

  • 0. ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ
  • 1. ACEGIKMOQSUWY, BDFHJLNPRTVXZ
  • 2. ADGJMPSVYBEHKNQTWZCFILORUX
  • 3. AEIMQUYCGKOSW, BFJNRVZDHLPTX
  • 4. AFKPUZEJOTYDINSXCHMRWBGLQV
  • 5. AGMSYEKQWCIOU, BHNTZFLRXDJPV
  • 6. AHOVCJQXELSZGNUBIPWDKRYFMT
  • 7. AIQYGOWEMUCKS, BJRZHPXFNVDLT
  • 8. AJSBKTCLUDMVENWFOXGPYHQZIR
  • 9. AKUEOYISCMWGQ, BLVFPZJTDNXHR

    Pressing an incorrect button will register a Strike, and the module will reset itself with a new letter.
