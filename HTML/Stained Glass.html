On the Subject of Stained Glass

They’re ancient, irreplaceable stained glass windows you absolute vandal!

  • The module shows a square window containing 25 stained glass panes.
  • Each pane has a rule with reference to the colour of either itself or one or more of its neighbours.
  • If the rule is true, click the pane to smash it.
  • Click all necessary panes to disarm the module.
  • Smashing a pane where the rule is false will cause a strike.
  • Adjacent refers to orthogonal panes.
  • Above and below refers to immediately diagonal panes (or the entire row where specified).
