On the Subject of Scramboozled Eggain

When a yolk goes too far...

Upon the module flipping over, six egg-shaped buttons, four LEDs, and a screen cycling through words encoded in eggzleglyphs will appear. The LEDs below the screen show which word in the sequence is being displayed. Pressing the six egg-shaped buttons in the correct order will disarm the module.

To decode the words, take each pair of eggzleglyphs and decode them using the table below, using columns for the first eggzleglyph and rows for the second.

The order in which to press the egg-shaped buttons will change based on the words shown in the display. The initial order is the white buttons in reading order followed by the brown buttons in reading order.

Each of the screens will show one of the words from the word list below. However, the order of the letters in each word has been scrambled up. Scramble the button order in the same way as the word. Continue scrambling the button order for each of the four words in the display.

Finally, press the six buttons in the final order. Only upon pressing all six buttons will your answer submit.
