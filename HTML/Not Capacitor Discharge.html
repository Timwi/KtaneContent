On the Subject of Not Capacitor Discharge

I’m going to guess that this is just meant to fill in the gaps, because otherwise this is some shoddy modification work.

This module consists of a capacitor, a lever, a light and a bar display on the left. The display has tick marks on the right. If the tick marks are on the left, you are looking at a different module.

When the module activates, a random 2-digit number will be displayed at the top.
Before the capacitor overloads, use the tables below to determine when to hold and release the lever before the bar on the left is filled.
Use the first row whose condition is true.

Warning: Pressing or releasing the lever at an incorrect time will cause the charge in the capacitor to increase.
