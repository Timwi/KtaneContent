On the Subject of Papyrus Tiles

Which is harder: Jumble or Crosswords?

GREETINGS HUMAN! YOU'RE GOING TO LOVE THIS PUZZLE! IT WAS MADE BY THE GREAT DR. ALPHYS! YOU SEE THESE TILES!? EACH COLOR HAS A DIFFERENT FUNCTION! START FROM THE LEFT OF THE GRID AND MAKE YOUR WAY TO THE RIGHT.
  • Red tiles are impassable. You cannot walk on them.
  • Orange tiles are orange scented. They will make you smell delicious!
  • Blue tiles are water tiles. If you smell like oranges, the piranhas will bite you*.
  • Purple tiles are slippery! You will slide to the next tile**. However, the slippery soap smells like lemons!!! Which piranhas do not like.
  • Green tiles will start an encounter with a monster that you must kill***.
  • Finally, pink tiles. They don't do anything. Step on them all you like.

*Getting bit will cause a strike, and move you at the position you were at before.

**If you slide your way onto a red tile, a strike will occur, and the module will reset.

***Killing a monster is done by waiting for the white bar to reach the center and pressing space. The closer to the center you are, the more damage you will do.
