On the Subject of Falsifying Info

Hey! Stop lying!

On the module, there will be 5 spheres. Every 5 seconds, one of the following things will happen every second and then a break will happen.

  • A sound playing.
  • A sphere changing colors.
  • The module background changing colors.

One of the changes will be lying. To figure out what is wrong, note down each change, and one of them will be changing throughout. Clicking the top left button will submit the change the module is on. If the click was performed on the correct second, then the module will solve. Otherwise, a strike will occur.
