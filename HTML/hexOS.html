Three-digit Addition

  • The module cycles through 10 3-digit numbers every second, with a pause to indicate the end of the sequence. Write down the entire sequence in order.

  • Modify it by adding each individual number with the absolute of the sum of +/- from the logic gates. Record only the digital root of each number.

  • Repeat the following procedure until the number is 3- or 4-digits long.
    • Split the sequence in groups of 6, add the left half of each group with the right half. Construct another sequence by linking all sums in preserved order into one big number.

    • If there are 1-5 leftover numbers that cannot be grouped together, generate a new sequence with leftover numbers repeating as long as non-leftover numbers. Add up both sequences' individual digits with each other and record the digital root of the sum as the new sequence.
  • If the number is a 4-digit number, drop the most significant digit.

Submitting

  • Holding the screen will make the top-right number turn pink. Releasing the screen will submit the corresponding digit for when the screen was held. Continuing to hold the screen will cancel the submission.
    • Hold and release the screen when the most significant digit of the 3-digit number matches the most significant digit on the module.

    • Hold and release the screen when the 2nd-most significant digit of the 3-digit number matches the 2nd-most significant digit on the module.

    • Hold and release the screen when the least significant digit of the 3-digit number matches the least significant digit on the module.
  • The uplink between the assigned vault and the system will be completed, and the module will be disarmed.
