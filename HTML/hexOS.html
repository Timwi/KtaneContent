Three-digit Addition

  • The module cycles through 10 3-digit numbers every second, with a pause to indicate the end of the sequence. Write down the entire sequence in order.

  • Modify all digits by adding each individual digit with the offset. Apply digital root* if any exceed 9.

    • Sequence Modification

    • Split the sequence in groups of 6. Add the left half of each group with the right half. Link all sums from left to right into one big number.

    • If groups of 6 can no longer be formed, remove the 1-5 rightmost digits and make a new sequence of itself repeating. Add both sequences' individual digits. If any exceed 9, apply the digital root*.

    • If the sequence is 6 digits or longer, repeat Sequence Modification.

*Digital root: Adding up all digits individually until one number is formed. Example: 555 = 5+5+5 = 15 = 1+5 = 6.

Submitting

  • If your final sequence is 4 digits long, subtract 1000.

  • Holding the screen will make the top-right number turn pink. Releasing the screen will submit the corresponding digit for when the screen was held. Continuing to hold the screen will cancel the submission.
    • Hold and release the screen when the left digit of the 3-digit number matches the left digit on the module.

    • Hold and release the screen when the middle digit of the 3-digit number matches the middle digit on the module.

    • Hold and release the screen when the right digit of the 3-digit number matches the right digit on the module.
  • The uplink between the assigned vault and the system will be completed, and the module will be disarmed.
