Upon a third tap on the module, it’ll display a 3x3 grid of colored squares, shuffling positions every second. Press the memorised color to continue. The module will then turn into one of its sub-modules below.

On the Sub-subject of Colorful Message

There will be 6 circular buttons with question marks on them, next to which is a circular display and a small button. Pressing the button will make the display show 6 colors, then letters will appear on the circular buttons. Use the memorised color along the top and the color of each display flashes to obtain 6 letters, which forms a word when put in the order of their appearance. Use the lettered buttons to type out the word. On a strike, the buttons will reset to their original state and a new sequence will flash.
