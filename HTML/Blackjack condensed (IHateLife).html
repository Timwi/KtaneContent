On the Subject of Blackjack

We are legally required to say that this module is not endorsing gambling and that you should gamble like there’s no tomorrow at your own risk.

  • Your cards will be notated as C1 to C5.
  • Your score is the sum of all of your cards. J = Q = K = 10. A = 1. If C1 = A, you may let it be equal to 1 or 11.
  • To solve the module, do not go over 21 and either:
    • Be equal to or higher than the dealer.
    • Make the dealer go over 21.

Determine C2

If C1 = A♠,

  • lit BOB, Check Blackjack.
  • SN vowel, C2 = 5♦, bet 1.
  • SN sum > 7, C2 = 7♠, bet 250.
  • Otherwise, C2 = 2♣, bet 10.

If C1 = K♦,

  • has an indicator that's not SND/IND, C2 = Q♥, bet 250.
  • D batt > 1, C2 = 9♠, bet 100.
  • Serial port, C2 = 3♦, bet 10.
  • Otherwise, C2 = 4♣, bet 1.

If C1 = 2♥,

  • ports > battery, C2 = 1♦, bet 100.
  • NSA indicator, C2 = 3♥, bet 10.
  • battery = 0, C2 = 7♣, bet 250.
  • Otherwise, C2 = 4♠, bet 1.

If C1 = 10♣,

  • SN has “CASINO”, C2 = 5♣, bet 100.
  • AA battery > 3, C2 = 3♥, bet 1.
  • Parallel port, C2 = 6♦, bet 250.
  • Otherwise, C2 = 1♠, bet 10.
