To solve the module, determine which three buttons to press using the capitalized Lean, and press them in an order given from your keyword.

Pressing the incorrect buttons will incur a strike, but the module will not reset.

FINDING BUTTONS

Your buttons will be known as a, b, and c.
Use the following steps to get your button numbers.
Modulo 16 (add or subtract 16 until within the range of 0–15) and remove decimals after each step. (i.e. 52.4 is 4). Add one to your final numbers.
Your numbers correspond to reading order buttons, top-left is 1.

A = First digit of SN B = Number of modules C = Last digit of SN

L and N are capital and A is lowercase. b = b / 3
L is capital and E is capital. c = a + b
E is lowercase. b = b - 7
