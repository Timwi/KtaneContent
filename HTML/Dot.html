Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod .

On the Subject of .

It’s all your fault.

This module will appear blank at first, then cycles five lines of Japanese text vertically when activated.

Hold the invisible scroller along the top left section of the module to manually cycle the text slightly faster. Hold the invisible scroller along the bottom left section of the module to manually cycle the text slightly slower. Note that this only works seconds after the activation of the module.

The lines shown are taken from the lyrics of one of three songs in Appendix ██████. Rearrange the lyrics based on their appearances in the song, then submit each lyric in this order.

The five lyrics are always consecutive, and they do not wrap around. Same lyrics can also show up at once, in which submitting either of them is acceptable if it’s correct.

To submit a lyric, press the invisible button along the right section of the module when the lyric shows up on the module. In the case of two lyrics showing at once, the module will submit the lowest lyric shown.

If a lyric is submitted correctly, its text color will be changed to green, otherwise it will briefly changed to red and give a strike. The colored text shows the lyric submitted.

When all five lyrics are submitted correctly, the module will play a short tune of the chosen song, indicating the module has been solved. The cycling lyrics would also stop when a certain lyric is shown on the module.
