Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Odd One Out

On the Subject of Odd One Out

You don’t belong here, there’s something wrong here...

For each stage, determine the Good Buttons, after which the only the non-Good Button is the Outlier, which should be pressed to complete the stage. Keep track of how many times each button has been pressed.

Stages 1-5: Depending on what the buttons show or/and what they say (when hovered over), determine the catergory in the table below they fall into. Refer to that category’s section to determine which buttons are the Good Buttons.

Stage 6: Each Good Button shares its press count with at least one other Good Button (whereas the Outlier has a unique press count).

Letter Codes (1-3 letters)

1-2 letters with first letter always capitalized:
If all buttons are valid chemical element symbols, then the Good Buttons all lie in the same row or the same column in the main body of the periodic table.
Otherwise, the Good Buttons are valid chemical element symbols (whereas the Outlier isn’t).

Note: the lanthanides and actinides (with the exception of Lanthanum (La) and Actinium (Ac)) are not considered as a part of the main body of the periodic table for this module (and are listed below it in this manual).
