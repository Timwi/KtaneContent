On the Subject of The Cornflower Button

It’s rude to stare.

There are three small arrows at the top left, bottom left, and bottom right of the module. They each point in random directions.

The top left points in three different directions, the bottom left points in five different directions, and the bottom right points in seven different directions.

Pressing the button will rotate the small arrows whose pivots are highlighted.

The large arrow protruding out of the button will point to a selectable object on a different module on the same side of the bomb. To change the highlights of the small arrows, hover over the selectable that the large arrow points at and deselect that module.

Occasionally, the arrow will point towards the alarm clock. Highlight the SNOOZE button and drop the alarm clock to change the highlights.

To disarm the module, spin the small arrows in such a way that they all point up.
