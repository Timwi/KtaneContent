Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod 3x3 Grid

On the Subject of 3x3 Grid

If there’s nothing here then do I do nothing or do everything?

  • A random combination of LEDs will appear on the 3x3 display to the right of the timer.*
  • If the last digit of the serial number is even, enter that combination onto the large 3x3 keypad.
  • If the last digit of the serial number is odd, enter the combination onto the large 3x3 keypad, but toggle the stage of each button.
    • Example: If the grid in the top-right has every square except the middle one on, only turn the middle one on for the actual input.
  • Make sure you have the proper input on the keypad by the time the timer runs out. If the timer runs out and the combination is wrong, you will receive a strike.

*There is also the possibility that the 3x3 display could be empty when the module activates. This is not a bug. Proceed as you normally would.
