On the Subject of Four Elements

This seems pretty dangerous — the elements are all unstable.

Select four different, orthogonally adjacent elements to make them disappear — causing any elements above to fall. Clear the board to solve the module. The two buttons next to the grid allow you to reset the board to its original state and undo the previous move you made, respectively.
