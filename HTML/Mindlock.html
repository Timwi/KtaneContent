On the Subject of Mindlock

When the phone unlock method is a game.

This module consists of 9 white buttons.

Press a sequence of 5 buttons in the correct order to disarm the module.

Pressing a button

When a button is pressed, it will turn yellow. When a total of 5 buttons are pressed, they will change their color again.

  • Red - This button is not used in the sequence.
  • Blue - This button is used in the sequence, but not at the correct place.
  • Green - This button is used in the sequence and at the correct place.

When all 5 buttons turn green after pressing them, the module will be disarmed.
