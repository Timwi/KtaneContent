On the Subject of Standard Crazy Talk

Blue red blue yellow... wrong question.

  • Instructions will be split across 5 buttons along the top of the module, with each button causing part of the instructions to appear when they are hovered over.
  • Simply follow the instructions to disarm the module. The instructions refer to the colored buttons. The buttons are either invisible areas in the 4 quadrants of the module, or the status light.
  • Bottom left, bottom right, top left, top right, and status light will be abbreviated to BL, BR, TL, TR, and SL respectively.
