On the Subject of Simon Strands

Oh yeah, we shippin' Simon to Brazil. Sucks to be that guy.

  1. Look up the transmitted morse word in the table on the next page.
  2. Convert the flashes of the diamond buttons into different colours with this row. Note this down.
  3. Move down a number of rows according to the color of the morse with the table on this page (wraps, mod 20).
  4. Set the frequency to this new row and then press the noted inputs to progress the module.
  5. If the module is left idle until the diamonds flash again, an incorrect button is pressed, or the wrong frequency is submitted, inputs are reset.
