On the Subject of Scattershot

This seems like way more buttons than anyone needs.

  • You must press buttons until you find and press the
    Key Button, while avoiding pressing incorrect buttons.
  • Each button has 4 traits:
    Its color (Blue, Red, White)
    Its shape (Triangle, Circle, Square)
    The letter written on it (U or Y)
    Whether the button is Dark or Light
  • When a button is pressed, all buttons of two traits chosen at random will illuminate. These traits do not necessarily match the Key Button.
  • Use the table below to find a trait that does belong to the Key Button,
    based on the two random traits that illuminate.
  • Press only buttons that possess all traits known to belong to the
    Key Button. Continue pressing buttons until the Key Button is found.
    Do not press the same button twice.
  • Begin by pressing any button.
