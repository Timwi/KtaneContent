These things don't even glow!

Using the non-center light bulb, locate the color combination on the table below and locate the sequence of the light bulb statuses on the intersection of the table. The center bulb will indicate on how you will submit your answer. If the center bulb is gray, invert every output on the gathered sequence. If not, submit the answer normally.
