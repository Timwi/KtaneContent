On the Subject of Light Bulbs

These things don't even glow!

There are three light bulbs available on the module. Each light bulb will contain a color. The center light bulb has 2 possible colors while the other light bulbs have 8 possible colors. Using the non-center light bulbs, locate the color combination on the table below and locate the sequence of the light bulb statuses on the intersection of the table. The center bulb will indicate how you will submit your answer. If the center bulb is gray, invert every output on the gathered sequence. If not, submit the answer normally.
