Remember this impossible thing? Now it’s on a bomb. Have fun.

Take your serial number to the table below and remove one of them depended on the DOWN face.

If the color of down face is... remove the... character from the serial

With the serial number and the colors of the Up, Left and Front face Shift Numbers gained from the above table (ex. 1st => +1, 2nd => +2, etc.), go to one of the subsections:

  • If your right face is Red, go to Section A and change the first 5 obtained moves from the table to their opposites (Exs. of opposites => D’ becomes D, R becomes R’, etc.).
  • If your right face is Green, go to Section A and reverse the order of all the moves (Ex. of reversing all moves => R’ L U D becomes D U L R’).
  • If your right face is Blue, go to Section A and input the moves normally.
  • If your right face is Yellow, go to Section B and change the first 5 obtained moves from the table to their opposites (Exs. of opposites => D’ becomes D, R becomes R’, etc.).
  • If your right Face is White, go to Section B and reverse the order of all the moves (Ex. of reversing all moves => R’ L U D becomes D U L R’).
  • If your right Face is Orange, go to Section B and input the moves normally.
