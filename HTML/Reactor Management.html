On the Subject of Reactor Management

Reminder that you are under a strict NDA.

Your provided explosive is powered by a prototype miniature nuclear reactor. By working with it, you are testing the prototype's capabilities.

The interface is internally attached to the reactor and a rechargeable power storage, which must remain within acceptable levels at all times.

  • If all power gets depleted, it will turn off shortly to reroute energy from a backup battery (expensive), and induce a strike.
  • If it exceeds capacity, the fuel rods have been predicted to melt, preventing use of recovery measures, and causing a destructive runaway reaction that will detonate the explosive.

Within the reactor, there are control rods and fuel:

  • Raising the control rods using the green button increases power production.
  • Lowering the control rods with the red button decreases power production down to a complete stop.

Once all modules have been solved, power production will cease, automatically shutting the reactor off safely.
