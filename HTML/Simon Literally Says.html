On the Subject of Simon Literally Says

Look, Simon’s tired of shrieking and screaming to get your attention. He just wants you to listen to him, and nobody else!

  • Press any button that fits the description given, but only when “Simon says:” immediately precedes it.
  • Otherwise, don’t press any buttons and let the timer run out.
NOTE: This module is light sensitive, and will temporarily stop working when the lights are out.
