Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Needy Pi

On the Subject of Needy Pi

o/d

Needy Pi is a Needy module, where your goal is to input five random digits of π. The module presents you with two displays and a number keypad. The top display shows the range of which digits you should input. The bottom display shows your current input and the keypad is just a keypad with numbers through 0 and 9.

Upon activation, the module will give you a random 5 digit range from 1 to 20 of π (first and last digits from the range are included). Input them to correctly in order to disarm the needy.

Π is a number with infinite decimals, but this module uses only the first 20 digits of it after the decimal. The first 20 digits of π read as follows: π ≈ 3.14159265358979323846. Ex. digits from 4-8 are 59265.

If you input an incorrect digit of π, you will get a strike. If the timer runs out before a correct answer is inputted, you will also get a strike.
