Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Cookie Clicker

On the Subject of Cookie Clicker

Where's the milk? There needs to be milk to drown the cookies in.

  • The module will consist of a picture of a cookie, a total cookie display and 2 different options: Buildings and Rebirth
  • Clicking the cookie will increase the total number of cookies. It will start off as 1 cookie per click (CPC).
  • Clicking on Buildings will bring up a new menu. Clicking on the top display will change the current building that is being upgraded. Holding the display for at least 1 second will buy that upgrade for amount showed on the middle display. There are also multipliers 1x, 10x, 100x, 1000x for faster upgrading. The bottom display will show how many total cookies there are.
  • Clicking on Rebirth will solve the module if you have a Cookie Jars Module and 4 batteries in 2 holders.
  • To solve the module, you will have to achieve a certain amount of total cookies and a certain achievement. In order to find out this information, follow the rules below.

Using the table below, figure out the total amount of cookies, the index for the building and the number in that list for that building. Add up each value from table including the duplicates. Ignore the states of the Indicators (lit/unlit). If after the modulo step that the value is equal to 0, change it to 1.
