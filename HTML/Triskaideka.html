Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Triskaideka

On the Subject of Triskaideka

Throw your superstitions out the window if you want to make it out of here alive.

To differentiate this module from other similar looking modules, highlights on this module will always be yellow instead of redish.

  • Number the squares and lines on the arc of the module from 1 to 13, starting at the top and going counter-clockwise.
  • The needle will rapidly move back and forth between a pair of markings.
  • Find the number in the triangle that is pointed at by two arrows along the top with the corresponding marking’s numbers. Call this number A.
  • Press the right red button will make the needle move between a new pair of markings. Use the new marking’s arrows on the right in the same way to get B. Press that button again and use the new pair and left arrows to get C.
