Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod :3

On the Subject of :3

https://i.imgur.com/G47RDJn.gif

The module has 4 buttons: :3, :], >:3, >:].

Every successful press will increase the number on the top screen by one.

  • If the number will be divisible by 100 after that, press >:],
  • Else, if the number will be divisible by 20 after that, press >:3,
  • Else, if the number will be divisible by 5 after that, press :],
  • Else, press :3.

Module accepts input when its LEDs are red. Successful presses award points, strikes take points away.

If after some press, the current point count is not less than the goal point count, the module will solve.

Be silly! And don't forget to respect the :] people too every now and then!
