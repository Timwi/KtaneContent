Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Port Check

On the Subject of Port Check

You did write down those ports, right?

The module has seven buttons, six that show the six port types, and one that says confirm.
The six ports can be selected or deselected by pressing them. After selecting the correct ports, press confirm.

Figuring out the correct ports

To figure out which ports to select, use the tables below.
Start at the first Table that has the name of a port that is on the bomb.
For that table, read the First Check from the top, and for the first
statement that is true, go to the Second Check for that statement.
After the Second Check, follow the instructions in the Instructions column.
