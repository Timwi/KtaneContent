On the Subject of Spell Me That

Is this the first module where the original manual is not in English?

This module has two screens, as well as a multitude of buttons.

To start, press the button at the top left of the module, with a triangle pointing to the right. A voice will announce a French word and a 60-second timer will start counting down. You can press the button as many times as you want.

Spell the spoken word using the letter buttons, then press the green button to submit your answer. The red button deletes your response entirely.

To add a letter with a grave, circumflex, or umlaut accent, first press the key with the desired accent, then the desired letter. Press the key with the accent again to return to the normal keyboard.

Letting the timer run out, or submitting an incorrect answer will cause a strike. Spell the word correctly to solve the module.
