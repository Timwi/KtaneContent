On the Subject of Pattern Wires

The shapes, the wires... what do they mean?

  • On the module is a wire panel. The wires come in different colours, and next to them are shapes.
  • To solve the module, find the correct shape pattern depending on the wire colours, then cut any wires that match the pattern.
  • Wires come in six colours: white, black, red, blue, green and yellow. Wire ordering begins with the first on the top.
  • Reverse the order of the pattern in the manual if the last digit of the serial number is odd.
  • You can press the status light to toggle colorblind mode.
