On the Subject of Needy Wires

Cut wire. Wrong wire. Boom.

Upon activation, a panel with three coloured wires will be presented.

Follow the rules below to determine the wire to cut.

  • If there are no red wires, cut the second wire.
  • Otherwise, if the last wire is white, cut the last wire.
  • Otherwise, if there is at least one blue wire, cut the last blue wire.
  • Otherwise, cut the first wire.
