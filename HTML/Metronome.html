On the Subject of Metronomes

If an IED can keep time, then so can you.

On the module is a dial metronome. Figure out what BPM the metronome is playing and set the dial correctly before the one minute timer expires.

While the module is in focus, a tick sound will play periodically at the rate of the chosen BPM. Defocusing from the module will mute the sound.

Pressing the left and right sides of the dial will decrease and increase the selected BPM respectively.

Failure to set the dial correctly will result in the module arming, denoted by a blinking LED. In this state, two consecutive correct answers are required to disarm the module.

Setting the dial incorrectly while the module is armed will incur a strike, and disarm the module.
