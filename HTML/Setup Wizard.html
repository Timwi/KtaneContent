  • Before you can start disarming this module, we need you to complete some preliminary steps to finish the installation process.

Page 2: Terms and Conditions

This page determines your password, which is a six-digit number. On the module, the password’s digits will be referred to as A-F respectively.

The terms and conditions page has a list of equations that limit your password to one possible number. Possible operations include:

  1. X + Y = # (addition)
  2. X - Y = # (subtration)
  3. X * Y = # (multiplication)
  4. X / Y = # (division)
  5. X || Y = # (concatenation)

However, two of the numbers after the “=” have been swapped. When you solve the system of equations and find your six-digit password, take note of the swapped numbers. The greatest swapped number is X, and the lowest is Y. Take the last digit of the serial number, modulo 5 plus 1, and use the corresponding operation from the list above on X and Y. Shift the digits in your password Z spaces to the right, where Z is the result, rounded down. This is your final password.

Page 3: Account Info

This page has spaces to enter your username and password. Type them in with the keyboard on the module and press “Finish” to submit. Entering an incorrect username or password will cause the module to administer a strike, but it will not reset.

When the module is installed, a large green button will appear on the module. Press this button to disarm the module.
