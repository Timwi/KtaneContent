Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Instant Insanity

On the Subject of Instant Insanity

This puzzle will fool you! You’ll be certain, and you’ll spin it around and realize... Whoops.

This module consists of 4 cubes, 6 rotation buttons, and a pedestal.

To disarm this module, rotate the cubes such that each of the four cardinal sides, when stacked, do not contain any duplicate colors. There may be multiple solutions. Select the pedestal to submit the current configurations.

An incorrect submission will result in a strike, causing the cubes to be unstacked in their current configurations.

Each cube can be selected to get a closer look at its corresponding faces.

Each rotation button will have an icon associated with the transformation applied to the selected cube. If no cube is selected, these buttons do nothing.
