Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Space Invaders Extreme

On the Subject of Space Invaders Extreme

Of all things, why is this a needy module?

Upon activation, this needy module will present a stage background from Space Invaders Extreme. To deactivate the module, select the name of the stage with that background by using the arrow buttons below the display, then pressing the stage name.
