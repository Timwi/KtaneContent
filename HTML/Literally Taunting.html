Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Literally Taunting

On the Subject of Literally Taunting

Nyaaah nyah, nyah NYAH nyaaaaaah

If the barrier is not in the standard orientation, you are looking at a different Needy Module.

This needy module features a sad emoji and a play button.

In order to deactivate the needy, press the play button to play a random baby insult that will make TracksJo-I mean, the emoji cry to sleep.

The emoji bends time and space, so there is a barrier around the emoji to prevent it from catastrophic disasters.

If you do not press the play button, the emoji will cry a river taller than the Nile River, and a strike will be given.

Wait... what do you mean the play button does nothing? Oh God... this can't be.

The barrier is in a different orientation, that means the emoji is free to run chaos!

Okay... the good thing is that the chaos is bound within the needy module and the chaos is limited to summoning five alternate reality copies called taunts.

These taunts increase starting at one every deactivation.

Press those taunts in any order to deactivate the needy, otherwise a strike will be given and I will run out of ideas for good KTANE mods.
