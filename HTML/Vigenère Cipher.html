On the Subject of Vigenère Cipher

Julius Caesar's little brother...by about 1600 years...

  • Take the first letter of the serial and the first letter of the code on the module, and translate them into numbers according to the Encryption Table below.
  • Add the two numbers together, and turn them back into a letter with the same table. If the numbers add up to something above 35, then subtract 36 until the output is in the table.
  • Repeat for the second letter of the code and the second letter of the serial, etc.
