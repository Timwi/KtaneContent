Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Kanji

On the Subject of Kanji

我は忍者になる世界平和のため！

You are presented with a four button module and a display screen. Each button has a unique symbol noted in a form of Japanese. This module consists of 3 stages, all incrementing in difficulty.
To solve this module, simply tap one of the four buttons for each stage that has an identical meaning to the display.
BUT: If there is a dragon energy on the bomb and the dragon kanji displays on the bottom right panel on stage 1, ignore the display and press the dragon kanji.
A strike will incur upon an incorrect selection as well as remain the same stage and reroll the answer for that stage.
