Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Overcooked

On the Subject of Overcooked

Let them cook.

This module has 3 displays with orders, a red trash button, a 3×3 grid of up to 9 ingredient buttons, a green serve button, a blue hands display, and up to 8 station buttons.

In this module you will be preparing/cooking the orders, in order, that are on the order displays. You can select an ingredient button to pick up the button’s displayed ingredient, select certain stations to prepare/cook the ingredient(s), and select the serve button to submit the food that you are holding in your hands.

The hands display will show at all times what you are holding and the trash button may be selected at anytime to permanently remove what you are holding.

This module will strike if you:

  • submit out of order
  • submit an incorrect order
  • allow something to burn/break*

*Something burns/breaks if the food is in a station that can burn/break for 5 seconds after it’s cooked/mixed.
