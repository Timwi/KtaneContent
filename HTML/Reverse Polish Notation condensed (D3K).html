Reverse Polish Notation (example):

3 5 + 7 2 - * postfix notation (RPN)

  • 3 5: (3, 5)
  • +: (3+5) = (8)
  • 7 2: (8, 7, 2)
  • -: (8, 7-2) = (8, 5)
  • *: (8*5) = (40) <-- answer

Lunar addition:

Take the largest digit in each position.

  • 726 + 351 = 756
