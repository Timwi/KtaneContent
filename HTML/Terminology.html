Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Terminology

On the Subject of Terminology

"Terminology" (noun) - The technical or special terms used in a business, art, science, or special subject

Defusing this module requires the expert to have a device that can connect to the World Wide Web.

  • This module contains 5 icons of other KTANE modules, and a definition from the Merriam-Webster Dictionary.
    • Such definition may be slightly paraphrased, but changes minimally from its original word.
  • Find the word that the displayed definition corresponds to in the Merriam-Webster Dictionary.
  • Exactly one of the displayed modules contains that word in their manual.
    • Words obviously can have multiple meanings, but there will never be contextual conflicts.
  • Press the icon of the one module where its manual contains that word.
  • Do this for 3 stages to solve the module.

Extra Specifications

  • This module queries icons from the Repository of Manual Pages. However, if the module fails to retrieve information from the Repository, the module will rely on built-in icons instead.
  • The border and text of the definition are colored based on the part of speech of the word: red represents nouns, yellow represents verbs, green represents adjectives, and blue represents adverbs.
  • The list of words can be found in Appendix WORDS.
