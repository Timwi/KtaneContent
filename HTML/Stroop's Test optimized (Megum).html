For those, who are familiar with the original manual.

Last SN#: even - text color, odd - word.

First SN#: even - top, odd - bottom.

Strike resets to stage 1. Strike conditions:

  • Wrong answer.
  • This question was answered before.
  • Answer 3 = Answer 2.

The following table tells you "good" (but not neseserally the best) sequence of questions to answer based on SN.
