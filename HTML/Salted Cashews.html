On the Subject of Salted Cashews

You're gonna love my nuts!

Eltrick felt like snacking and bought a pack of salted cashews, however some of these cashews are awful. To solve the module, send five actually edible cashews to Eltrick with the top button. Sending a nut of bad quality will incur a strike and make Eltrick feel done with snacking as much as eating two good cashews would.

A nut is considered of proper quality if:

  • The amount of salt on it is within an approved range.
  • The roast level of it is within an approved range.
  • The difference in salt on its sides is within an approved range.

The middle button can be used to flip the nut and see its back and properly assess the saltiness.

These approved ranges depend completely on Eltrick's mood. The bottom button can be used to send the cashew to a dummy Eltrick who will simulate the response you'd get from the real deal.
