On the Subject of Poker

Poker? I hardly know her!

The module will display a single face-up card.

You must push two of the buttons and one of the face-down cards in order to disarm it.

Use the given card to determine which of the four tables you should use.

Use the table to determine the appropriate button to press.

Your opponent will then respond. Use the two-way table to determine whether the response is the “truth” or a “bluff”.

Your opponent will then make a bet. Use the betting rules to determine which of the four final cards you should press.

Go left if it’s false, and right if it’s true.

Betting Rules

$25 Bet:

If the 1st card is red and there is a lit BOB indicator, press the 4th card.
Otherwise, if your opponent said “Awful play!” and the starter card was the Ace of Spades, press the 1st card.
Otherwise, if there is an unlit FRQ indicator and the 4th card is black, press the 2nd card.
Otherwise, if diamond ≥ 1 and your opponent said “Really?”/“Really, really?”, press the 3rd card.
Otherwise, if the 4th card is a spade and there are 4 > batteries, press the 3rd card.
Otherwise, if the 3rd card is a diamond and the 2nd card is not a club, press the 2nd card.
Otherwise, if your opponent said “Are you sure?” and the starter card was the Two of Clubs, press the 1st card.
Otherwise, if the starter card was the Five of Diamonds, press the 4th card.
Otherwise, if the 2nd card is a club and RJ-45 = 0, press the 2nd card.
Otherwise, press the 1st card.

$50 Bet:

If your opponent said “Sure about that?” and the 4th card is a heart, press the 1st card.
Otherwise, if clubs = 0 and the starter card was the Two of Clubs, press the 3rd card.
Otherwise, if a heart appears anywhere above a spade and diamonds = 0, press the 4th card.
Otherwise, if the 1st card is a heart and the starter card was not the King of Hearts, press the 2nd card.
Otherwise, if your opponent said “Really, really?” and the 1st or 2nd card are hearts, press the 4th card.
Otherwise, if the starter card was the Five of Diamonds and there is a parallel port, press the 1st card.
Otherwise, if there is a lit TRN indicator and black ≥ 1, press the 2nd card.
Otherwise, if your opponent said “Terrible play!”, press the 3rd card.
Otherwise, if the digits of the serial number add up to less than 10 press the 1st card.
Otherwise, press the 3rd card.

$100 Bet:

If your opponent said “Really, really?”, press the 2nd card.
Otherwise, if your opponent said, “Really?”, press the 4th card.
Otherwise, if there are no D batteries and the starter card was the Ace of Spades, press the 1st card.
Otherwise, if the digits of the serial number add up to a prime number and heart ≥ 1, press the 4th card.
Otherwise, if a club and a spade appear and your opponent said “Sure about that?”, press the 3rd card.
Otherwise, if a club and a spade appear next to each other, press the 2nd card.
Otherwise, if there is an unlit MSA indicator, press the 1st card.
Otherwise, if diamond ≥ 1, press the 3rd card.
Otherwise, if your opponent said “Awful play!”, press the 4th card.
Otherwise, press the 2nd card.

$500 Bet:

If club > 1, press the 3rd card.
Otherwise, if the serial number contains a vowel and spade ≥ 1, press the 2nd card.
Otherwise, if ports = 0 and heart ≥ 1, press the 1st card.
Otherwise, if there are no red cards, press the 4th card.
Otherwise, if your opponent said “Are you sure?”, press the 4th card.
Otherwise, if lit indicators = 0 and the 1st card is a heart, press the 3rd card.
Otherwise, if unlit indicator ≥ 1 and the 2nd card is a club, press the 2nd card.
Otherwise, if your opponent said “Really?” and there are no black cards, press the 1st card.
Otherwise, if D battery > 1, press the 3rd card.
Otherwise, press the 4th card.
