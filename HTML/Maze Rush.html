Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Maze Rush

On the Subject of Maze Rush

I wonder what the mouse in all of these maze modules thinks about mazes.

When this module activates, it will present a 3×3 maze. Navigate the circle to the star using the arrow buttons or WASD keys: doing so will add five seconds (twenty on Twitch Plays) to the module’s timer and generate a new maze.

When you walk into a wall, you will receive a warning and a twenty second penalty (on Twitch Plays, there are no penalties). Completing a maze resets your warning counter.

The module will strike and deactivate if you either run out of time, or receive two warnings in a row.
