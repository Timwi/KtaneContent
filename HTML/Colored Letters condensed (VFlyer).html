Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Colored Letters

On the Condensing of Colored Letters

Multiple colors and letters ready to give you a hard time.

4 - 6 colored letters will appear on each button. There will be a pause between each sequence.

Select the buttons with scores from largest to smallest. Ties can be pressed in any order.

An incorrect press will generate a new set of letters and colors in addition to striking.

On the table on the next page, the left column for each color is the starting score with the right column representing the modifier to the score for that button.
