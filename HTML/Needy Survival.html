Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Needy Survival

On the Subject of Needy Survival

I was asked to make this manual 2 years ago. I said to be patient for a reason.

This module will solve at the last second of the bomb's timer, and never at any other point in time for any other reason. When the module solves, it's text will change from "Needy Survival" to "You survived!".

Just keep disarming needies, the mission will end eventually.
