On the Subject of GeoGuessr

The trees look Polish?!

This module will display a Google Street View panorama and a compass.

If available, movement controls will be shown on the screen. The arrows on the sides of the screen will pan the camera in that direction, the plus and minus buttons will zoom in and out, the flag button will reset the panorama to its original position, and pressing the compass will make the panorama face north.

To solve the module, enter the correct country code of where the panorama is taken in. To submit a guess, press the Pin button, spell out the correct country code, then press GUESS.

To return to Street View, press the fullscreen button.

If the module fails to download the panorama, then the module will use a limited supply of panorama screenshots, and panning & zooming will be disabled.

Country metas

Below is a list of metas per country and how it might look in Street View.

At least one meta will show up per country. Some countries may share the same meta.

Locations will be panned to have the country’s meta(s) already in frame.

If the first column has an image of a two way traffic sign, that means that country drives on the left side of the road. Otherwise, they’ll drive on the right.

Any metas with a “+” means it needs to have that combination in order for it to be that country.

If you changed the mod’s “Map” setting from “KTaNE Manual” to something else, then locations will not be based on the metas below.
