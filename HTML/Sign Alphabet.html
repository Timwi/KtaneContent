On the Subject of Sign Alphabets

Sign language should be in every school's curriculum.

When this needy activates, 1 of 2 things may happen:

Arrows appear near the hand sign display:

A letter shows up on the bottom display. Use the arrows to cycle through the different signs to locate the one corresponding to the letter and press the sign display to submit the displayed sign.

The hand signs are NOT cycling in alphabetical order, however the order they are in stays the same at all times.

Arrows appear near the letter display:

A hand sign shows up on the top display. Use the arrows to cycle through the alphabet and find the one corresponding to the sign. Press the letter display to submit that letter.

Take note that:

Submitting an answer will deactivate the module. If the sign and the letter do not match, a strike is incurred.

If time runs out before you submit your answer, you will get a strike, even if the displayed sign and letter match.
