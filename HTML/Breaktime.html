Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Breaktime

On the Subject of Breaktime

Another 9-5 .-.

The module displays a random number between 1 - 10 in the bottom right of the module.

This number is the amount of hours you are working for your current work shift, which also means you get a certain number of breaks.

Using this number, simply press one of the 8 buttons that correspond to it in accordance with the table below.

Hours Breaks
1, 2, 3 0 Breaks
4 1 Break
5 1 & 1/2 Breaks
6 2 Breaks
7 2 & 1/2 Breaks
8 3 Breaks
9 3 & 1/2 Breaks
10 4 Breaks
