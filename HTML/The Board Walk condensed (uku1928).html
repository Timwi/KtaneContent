Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Board Walk

On the Subject of Blissfully Walking Through The Board Walk

Capitalism.

  • If you’ve landed on every property in a color set (excluding current stage), the rent payment for that color set is doubled.
  • Every time after you pay rent on a property, you add a house to that property. If the property already has 4 houses, replace them with a hotel instead. If it has a hotel, don’t build anything.
  • If you roll doubles three stages in a row, you are sent to jail.
  • After all other non-ignored modules have been solved, Input the last five digits of the absolute value of your accumulated debt. The bottom display increments the last digit by 1, looping from 9 to 0. The switch shifts the number left. The token display submits your answer.
  • If the module strikes upon submitting the wrong answer, the leftmost wrong digit and after will be marked as red while other digits will show as green. Pressing the stage indicator will then enter the recovery mode.
