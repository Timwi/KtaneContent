On the Subject of Nailing Dates

DISCLAIMER: This does NOT work in real life. Do not attempt this with your date.

What to do in this cheat-sheet:

  • Determine whether to act or gift first using table A.
  • Get which table to use in the pages 2 to 5 depending on the time the module was activated.
  • Use the row in the table that has the most conditions met.
  • Determine if the activation time of the module is considered early, middle or late for the actions with table B.
  • In the correct order, the defuser should then perform the sequence of actions and (if applicable) gift an item.

What to note:

  • "SN" means serial number in the conditions.
  • A module name mentioned in the conditions (in italics) means that module is present on the bomb.
  • If there are no met conditions, use the third row in the table.
  • If there is a tie for the conditions, use the second row out of the rows that have the most conditions met.
