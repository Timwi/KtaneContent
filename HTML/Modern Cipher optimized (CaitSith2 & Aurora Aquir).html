On the Subject of Cracking Modern Cipher

Edgework? Solved module counts? Strikes? Who needs any of that.

  • You are given an encrypted word. Decrypt it, write it in the second box and press OK to solve the module.
  • To decrypt the word start by finding the differences between the letters. Look up the 1st letter as the row (“from”) and the 2nd as the column (“to”). This will result in a number. Do the same for the 2nd and 3rd, 3rd and 4th, etc.
  • Once you have your numbers, look for them in table 2 to find your decrypted word.
