On the Subject of Emoticon Math

😄😎🤞🍌

Decipher the emojis on the display into numbers and a symbol and solve the answer to the question. Enter the answer with the keypad and press ‘=’ to submit it. Use ‘-’ to toggle the negative sign for negative answers. There’s no delete button so press those buttons carefully!

For example: 🍔💎😄 translates to 1+1
