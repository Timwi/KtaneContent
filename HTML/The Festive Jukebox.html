On the Subject of The Festive Jukebox

What goes ho-ho-ho-BOOM?

  • The module consists of a festive jukebox with three lyrics and three vinyl buttons.
  • Use the given lyrics to work out which Christmas song has been selected.
  • Enter the song lyrics in the order they appear.
  • Certain words may appear in multiple songs, but all three will only appear in any one song.
  • If a word appears more than once in a song, use its first instance.
