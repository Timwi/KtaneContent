Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Q

On The Rewording of Q

Somehow, lost more braincells over an IQ testing game.

Press the Q on the module.

An image will be revealed upon pressing Q. Use your keyboard to type in a series of letters that the image corresponds to. This may be a single letter.

For example, “Tea” = T.
