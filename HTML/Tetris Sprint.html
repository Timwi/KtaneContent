Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Tetris Sprint

On the Subject of Tetris Sprint

Usain Tetris? Tetris Bolt?

  • Play Tetris.
  • This module requires a keyboard to solve.
  • Controls:
    • A / ← : Move the piece one space left.
    • D / → : Move the piece one space right.
    • W / ↑ : Rotate the pieece 180°
    • S / ↓ : Move the piece one space down. (soft drop)
    • Space: Drop the piece to the bottom of the board. (hard drop)
    • Z: Rotate the piece 90° counterclockwise.
    • X: rotate the piece 90° clockwise.
  • To activate the module, press any of the keys in the controls. Upon activating, a timer will start.
  • Pieces will not fall with time. They will only fall upon soft or hard drops.
  • To solve the module, clear 40 rows. There's no time limit to do so.
  • If the board fills up, the board will reset, a strike will be incurred, your score will drop by 200 points, and you will be penalized by 20 seconds.
