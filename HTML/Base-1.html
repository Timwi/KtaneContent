Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Base-1

On the Subject of Base-1

"I don't know what you're talking about... it is NOT a tally mark system!" - Anonymous.

  • This module consists of 9 buttons and a screen.
  • The buttons consists of numbers between 1-9.
  • The screen will display a number between 1-9 in base-1.
  • Add all the ones in the display to turn a number from Base-1 to Base-10.
  • To solve the module, press the button that has the decimal digit of the number shown in base-1.
  • Pressing an incorrect button will cause a strike.

How to turn a number from Base-1 to Base-10:

  1. Find a power of one that works.
  2. Cry.
