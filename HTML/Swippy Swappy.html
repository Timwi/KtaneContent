On the Subject of Swippy Swappy

They got a brand new dance! It's called the Cupid Shuffle!

  • Once this needy activates, it never deactivates. The curtains will open, revealing 5* blocks.
  • When you turn the bomb so the module isn't visible, two blocks will switch positions.
  • When the blocks are sorted in ascending order, press the button.
  • Turning away from the module when the list is sorted without pressing the button or pressing the button when the list is not sorted will incur a strike.

*The amount of blocks can be changed in mod settings.
