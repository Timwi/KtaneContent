On the Subject of Letter Math

These letters don't look right to me.

This module consists of a display with an equation on it and three numbered buttons. Press the correct button to solve the module. Pressing the incorrect button will result in a strike.

To find the correct button, convert the letters on the display into numbers (A=0, B=1, etc). Use these numbers to solve the equation. To solve the module, press the button which matches the solution of the equation.
