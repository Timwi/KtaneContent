On the Subject of the Porta CM

A machine that knows no bounds when it comes to ciphers.

Do the following instructions:

  • Use the Nth letter of the keyword as the row in the table below.
  • If the Nth letter of the encrypted word is between A-M, use the letter as the column for the chart. Then the intersection of the row and column of the 2 letters will give you a decrypted letter.
  • Otherwise, find the Nth letter of the encrypted word in the same row as the Nth letter of your keyword. The column of that letter will give you a decrypted letter via the A-M row of the chart.

Do this for each letter of the encrypted word and the keyword to get a decrypted word.

If you reach the end of the keyword, treat it as if it wraps around to the beginning of the keyword.
