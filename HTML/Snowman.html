On the Subject of Snowman

Fanfact: In Japan, snowmen look a little different — they usually have two snowballs instead of three!

You must build a snowman. To roll a ball, you must press the module and hold it for the required number of seconds for each ball.

The holding time for snowballs is calculated as:

  • For the Biggest snowball — current day of month modulo 5 + 3.
  • For the Middle snowball — count solvable modules modulo 3 + 2.
  • For the Smallest snowball — count batteries modulo 2 + 1.
ATTENTION! The module uses real time, not the time on the bomb. Maximum deviation from the ideal is 0.33s. Example: if you need roll the ball for 6s, then correct time must be in range from 5.67s to 6.33s.
