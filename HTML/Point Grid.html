On the Subject of Point Grid

I’m just trying to find the bathroom...

  • This module consists of a 6×6 grid of numbers, along with a row and column of arrow buttons at the top and right of said grid.
  • To solve the module, click the green button in the middle once the arrows have been put into the correct orientation.
  • Clicking the green button when the arrows are not in the correct orientation will incur a strike.

Rules for Arrows:

  • All arrows must be pointing towards at least one cell in the number grid.
  • The numbers in each cell represent how many arrows are pointing to that cell.
