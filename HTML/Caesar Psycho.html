Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Caesar Psycho

On the Subject of Caesar Psycho

Veni, veni, veni

The module displays five letters on its upper screen.
Use the transformations corresponding to the colours of each letter in the right table to obtain the original sequence of letters.

Use the left table with the current stage number to find the first letter of the target word.

Caesar shifting the first letter of the target word by the alphabetic positions of each letter in the original sequence one after another yields the other letters of the target word.

Enter the target word into the module using the module's keyboard.
The colour transformations also apply the letters that are entered.

Submitting the correct target word will advance the module to the next of three stages, scramble the keyboard, and change its colouring.

The red button can be pressed at any time to clear the entry.
