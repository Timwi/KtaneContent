On the Subject of Codenames

The sheet’s good. Goodsheet. Cheat good’s the sheet.

  • Find the flashing sequence of black/pink in the left column of the following table (K = black, I = pink).
  • Use the corresponding characters in column 2 to assign colors blue (B)/red (R)/black (K)/neutral (N) to the words shown on the module.
  • Out of columns 3–5, use the first applicable column to determine which category to use.
  • Observe the background of the module (blue or red) to determine which color word must be submitted.
  • Submit every word that is both the correct color and in the correct category.
