On the Subject of Mask Identification

Your flavor text should go here.

Press the gray mask to reveal a mask. It will stay on screen for 7.5 seconds before reverting back to the original image.

Once the timer is over, you will be able to type in the text box (with either the on screen buttons or your keyboard). Type in the name of the mask exactly how it appears in the manual and press Enter to submit.

Submitting the correct name will proceed you to the next stage. Submit 3 mask names correctly in a row to solve the module. Submitting an incorrect name will cause a strike, and the module will reset back to the first stage.
