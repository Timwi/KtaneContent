Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Thirty One

On the Subject of Thirty One

Unlike other modules, you want to hit this one.

  • Convert the suit of the first card into a direction, then find the location in the grid below where the rank of the second card lies in that direction from the first.
  • Using the second card's suit, determine the next card.
  • The defuser must press Hit unless all the cards together goes over 31.
    (J is 11, Q is 12, K is 13, A is 1)
  • Otherwise, the defuser must press Stand.
  • Upon pressing Stand, modify the compass rose of suits using the suit of the last card dealt this stage. This resets on a strike.
