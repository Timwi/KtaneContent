On the Subject of Yoshi Egg

It’s just an egg! How is it supposed to be that difficult!?

On the module consists of an egg flashing 7 colors, some of which may repeat. There will be a long rest at the end of the sequence to distinguish between the start and end.

Convert the flashing colors into their respective numbers using the table provided. If colorblind mode is enabled, the letters underlined will be used to represent that color.

Color: Num:Color: Num:Color: Num:
Green 1Pink 5Black 9
Red 2Cyan 6Gray 10
Blue 3Purple 7Brown 11
Yellow 4Orange 8Tan 12

Take the sum of the numbers and modulo it by 60 by adding or subtracting 60 until it is within the range from 0 to 59.

Press the egg when the seconds digits of the bomb's timer display that result.
