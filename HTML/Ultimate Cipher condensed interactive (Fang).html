Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod The Ultimate Cipher

On the Subject of the Ultimate Cipher

All ciphering techniques are required to solve this module.

See Appendix W0RD5 for word list reference.

  • On the first page of the module, the top screen should show a 6 symbol word that has been ecrypted.
  • The module has a total of 7 pages, 1 for the first screen, and 2 for each colored cipher.
  • Follow the nessecary steps below to get your decrypted word to submit.

    • Step 1: Pigpen Cipher

    Convert the 6 symbols on the top screen of page 1 to letters using the chart below:

    On the Subject of the White Cipher

    Original White Cipher manual can be found here.

    This cipher contains Base Caesar, Grille Transposition and Sean Cipher.

    Edgework:

    1. Step 1: sum of the alphanumeric positions of the letters of the encrypted word, modulo 8 the sum then add 2.
    2. Step 2: rotate 90 degrees clockwise X times, where X is the number of ports.
      3. X   |   X|    | XX 
         X|X |X X |    
       X X|   |X   |  X 
          | XX|   X|X   
    3. Step 3: number of lit indicators is even

    Inverted White Cipher Order:

    • Step 3: Sean Cipher
    • Step 2: Grille Transposition
    • Step 1: Base Caesar Cipher

    Original Black Cipher manual can be found here.

    This cipher contains Digrafid, Railfence Transposition and Enigma Cipher.

    Edgework:

    1. Step 1: Alphanumeric position of the first letter of the serial number is odd

    3. Step 2: number of ports, modulo 4, plus 2.
