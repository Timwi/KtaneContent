On the Subject of Speak English!

So. Many. Languages.

To complete this module, you must complete three stages. Each stage will have one english word or phrase on the display and foreign language words and phrases on the button. You must click the one and only one foreign word or phrase that is the translated version of the english one, which you, the defuser, know. Below is a list of foreign words that show up in this module
