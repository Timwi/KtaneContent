On the Subject of I'm Not a Robot

Look at these curvy letters. Much curvier than most letters, wouldn't you say? No robot could ever read these.

At the top of the module will be a short set of instructions that reads "Select all bombs with a [module name]."

Select every image in which that module is visible. The image will disappear and a new image will take its place.

Repeat this process until none of the remaining images include the described module, and then select "VERIFY."

Note: If the instructions say "wires," BE CAREFUL: it's referring to the module "Wires," not just any wire-related modules.
