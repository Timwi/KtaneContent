Appendix Trunic:

Trunic is a phonetic language based on English. The outer sides of the character represent a vowel sound, while the inner lines represent a consonant sound. The consonant sound is pronounced then the vowel, unless there is a circle underneath the character which then the reverse happens (unused in the module). Below is a table of all the consonants and vowels used in the module.

Consonants
B as in Bravo
D as in Delta
F as in Foxtrot
Vowels
A as in Glass
EH as in The
ERE as in Air
