On the Subject of (Cruel) Dial Trials

speen

Align the dials, such that all four quadrants have the same total value, where ⬤ = 1 and ⯁ = 5, then press the centre button. Submitting an incorrect permutation of the dials will result in a strike.

Dial Trial has two dials, whereas Cruel Dial Trial has three.
