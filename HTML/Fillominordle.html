On the Subject of Fillominordle

You haven’t seen my true colors.

  • Guess the 5x5 Fillomino shown on the module within seven guesses.
    • A Fillomino puzzle is a grid that is partitioned in such a way that every partition is of unique size to each neighbor.
  • When a tile turns grey after submission, that means that is no more of that number in the puzzle.
  • When a tile turns yellow after submission, that means that number is present in the puzzle, but you put it in the wrong square.
  • When a tile turns green after submission, that means that square has the correct number.
  • Failure to guess the Fillomino will reset the module and generate a new puzzle.
