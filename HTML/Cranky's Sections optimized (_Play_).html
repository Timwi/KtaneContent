For those familiar with the original manual.

Throughout the entirety of this module, keep track of totals Notes and Points.
Every time you solve a non-ignored module (i.e. for every stage of this module):

  • If the displayed Percentage is 100%, add 250 to Notes and add 0 Points.
  • Otherwise,
    • Stage Points = Misses + 0.25 * Barlies.
    • Add (Stage Points/(1 - 0.01 * Percentage)), rounded up, to Notes.
      • Percentage is the displayed percentage with the percent sign dropped.
    • Add Stage Points to Points.

After solving all non-ignored module, submit ((Notes - Points)/Notes), rounded down to 2 decimal places.
