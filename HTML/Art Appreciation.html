On the Subject of Art Appreciation

"This is art. Please appreciate it."

Look at the art.

Looking away from the art hinders your appreciation of the art; look directly at the art to appreciate it.

Looking at the art from afar hinders your appreciation of the art; get up close to the art to appreciate it.

Once you have sufficiently appreciated it, you will feel enlightened. (And the module will solve).
