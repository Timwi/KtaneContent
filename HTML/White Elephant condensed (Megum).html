On the Subject of White Elephant

From the creators of Red Herring

For those who are familiar with the original manual.

  • Press the elephant.
  • Note down 3 presents' colours (C1, C2, C3) and 3 numbers (N1, N2, N3) in order.
  • Concatenate N1, N2, N3 to make first rule seed (RS1) and visit that ruleseed.
  • Use the venn diagram to determine the present to give.
  • Note down names and their IDs (aka ruleseeds where ??? is RS1).
  • Only one name will be present across any of the 3 RSs' tables, select that person, remember their position P (1-3).
  • Depending on the received present's colour Cx visit ruleseed Nx
  • Click on your present when the last digit of the timer is at position P in the table.
  • Pressing the wrong present, the wrong person, or your present at the wrong time will result in a strike and the entire module resets.
