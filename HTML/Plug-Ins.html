On the Subject of Plug-Ins

When all else fails, just jam the plug into the socket. Works every time.

This module shows a duplex receptacle with two oddly-shaped outlets with a green button in the bottom middle.

When the green button is pressed, five buttons with labels will appear on the module.

The labels are the names of different devices. The green button as well as the duplex receptacle will disappear.

Select the device that can fit into either of the receptacles to solve the module.

If an incorrect device is chosen or 3 seconds passed since pressing the green button, a strike will be issued, the module will reset and a new duplex receptacle will be shown.

A total of 3 stages are necessary to disarm this module.
