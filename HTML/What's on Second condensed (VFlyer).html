On the Subject of What’s on Second

You know Who’s on First. But have you ever thought about the guy before him? The guy that almost won but didn’t? Well, good luck, ‘cause he’s having his revenge... Well... That was easier than I thought.

  1. Read the display/determine the display text's color to determine which button label and text color to use. Use the table provided to determine what button positions you must use (in reading order).
  2. Combine the result gathered from Step 1. Use the same table and the gathered combination to locate the list that you will be using, denoted in parentheses.
  3. Gather all the button labels on the module, except the button label that was gathered on Step 1. Using the given list plus the button labels you have gathered, locate the the button label that is in the middle of the list. That is the correct button that must be pressed.
  4. Repeat until you solve 3 stages in a row. Striking resets your solves.

Each cell in the next two tables provided is formatted as the following:
