Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Regular Show

On the Subject of Regular Show

You know who else is one button press away from exploding?

Something’s gone horribly wrong at the park—again. Garrett Bobby Ferguson Jr. has returned, and this time he’s found a way to resurrect some of Regular Show’s most infamous villains from all 8 seasons! Three different locations are overrun with chaos, each with their own resurrected boss. To defeat each boss, you must identify the character best equipped to take them down.

  • You will be presented with a location, a boss, and 3 random characters from the Regular Show universe
  • Every character has a base power level determined in Table 1: Base Power. Find the intersection of that character and the background location to determine base power level
  • Then, modify each character’s base power by applying each true condition for that character found in Table 2: Modifiers to obtain their final power level
  • Finally, press the button containing the character with the highest final power level. If multiple characters have the highest final power level, choose either of them
  • HOWEVER, before selecting a character, check to see if any special conditions apply to the given event, located in Appendix: Special Conditions. Apply the effects of all true conditions after finding each character’s final power level
  • Select a correct character for 3 locations to solve the module
