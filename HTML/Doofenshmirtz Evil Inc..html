On the Subject of Doofenshmirtz Evil Inc.

Malifishmirtz Evil Incorporated, but not really a corporation because corporations haven't been invented yet so it's more like a guild or a tradesman association!

This module is themed after Dr. Doofenshmirtz's company from Disney's Phineas and Ferb. It has 2 arrow buttons, a play button, a display with a number between 1 and 3, and two images. Each image can be a Dr. Doofensmirtz "-inator" or a trap to capture Perry the Platypus.

To solve this module you must submit the correct jingle of Dr. Doofensmirthz's company for the day. Use the arrow buttons to change which jingle is selected on the display and press the play button to play it. Holding down the play button will submit the selected jingle.

If the wrong jingle is submitted then the module will record a strike and the module WILL reset.

Determining The Jingle

Find the two images in the table below, and draw a line from the center of one to the other. Find the midpoint of this line, and the correct jingle will be the one which is closest to the midpoint. Jingles in the below table are represented as different characters, use the second page to determine which jingles are which characters.
