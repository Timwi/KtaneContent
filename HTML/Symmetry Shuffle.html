Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Symmetry Shuffle

On the Subject of Symmetry Shuffle

It’s not a big truck. It’s a series of tubes!

  1. Only one of the sets below contains all five displayed suits.
  2. The containers at the bottom of the module must be filled with the chemicals that correspond to the order of the suits in the set.
  3. The buttons, from top to bottom. perform the following functions:
    • Swap the two upper filtration units.
    • Flip the middle filtration unit horizontally.
    • Flip the middle filtration unit vertically.
    • Swap the lower two filtration units.
  4. Pulling the lever dispenses the chemicals from the top of the module, travelling down through the filtration units, and filling the containers.
    If any container is filled with the wrong chemical; the module is reset, generating a new set of suits to display.
