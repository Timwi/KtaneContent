On the Subject of Lime Arrows

The point was blown away by the wind. Check the weather forecast next time!

On the module are 4 directional buttons, a 6-letter word, and a display screen in the middle.

If the buttons are not Lime, you’re looking at a different module.

The center screen always displays your current location in the maze below.
To solve the module, locate the exit and reach it using the directional buttons.

But be careful! After every move, the wind will push you in a certain direction, forcing you to move once.

Find the 6-letter word displayed on the module in the table below. Beside every word is specified a direction [Up/Down/Left/Right].
This direction indicates where the wind will push you. After each press, you move one cell in the maze, then the wind pushes you and then you move in the table below.
Move one cell down in the table if the serial number has a letter in common with “WIND”, otherwise move one cell to the right. The table wraps around.

ACTIVE [R] ENERGY [U] INSANE [D] MELODY [R] PORTAL [L] TECHNO [U]
ALWAYS [D] EXPERT [R] ISLAND [U] NARROW [L] QUARKS [U] TOGGLE [R]
BIONIC [L] FAULTY [D] JETWAY [R] NOISES [D] RECOIL [L] UPLOAD [U]
CANYON [D] GLOBAL [U] KARATE [L] OFFSET [R] REPLAY [D] WORLDS [L]
CLOVER [U] GRAVEL [L] LETHAL [D] OUTPUT [U] STREAK [R] YOGURT [D]
DECODE [L] HORNET [R] LOUNGE [U] PIGEON [L] STRING [D] ZENITH [R]

The maze has 4 possible exits, however only one is active at a time. The exits are denoted as green dotted lines on the maze. The direction associated with the word written on the module determines the correct exit.

Trying to cross a line in the maze (including movements from the wind) will result in one strike per input. In case of an invalid move, the wind will still try to push you if there isn’t a wall blocking you.
