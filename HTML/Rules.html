On the Subject of Rules

Skimming has never been harder.

  • The middle text, when pressed, will display a rule that the defuser must follow. Following a rule correctly will display another rule. There is a limited amount of time the defuser can follow rules.
  • The minimum amount of rules that need to be followed can be found in the table below. However, if this is the last unsolved module on the bomb, the required amount of rules needing to be followed is 1. This has the highest priority.
    % of Bomb Time Remaining Rules Needed to Follow
    %100 - %80 20
    %79 - %60 16
    %59 - %40 12
    %39 - %20 8
    %19 - %0 4
    There is one minute or less remaining. 1
