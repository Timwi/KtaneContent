On the Subject of Stenography

Typing at an astonishing one word per bomb!

  • Press the * key when the last digit of the timer is equal to the number of Stenography modules on the bomb.
  • N = (product of all digits in the S#) % 64. Take the Nth word from the table below in reading order (0-indexed). Input the stroke of the found word.
    • The table on the next page contains the 0-indexed position and stroke of every word from the table below.
  • The module’s display will now contain the First word (written in a 3-pixel-tall font).
  • R = (batteries) % 7 + 1. D = (battery holders) % 7 + 1.
  • Go R words right and D words down from the First word in the table below (wrapping around if needed) to obtain the Second word.
  • Go R words right and D words down from the Second word in the table below (wrapping around if needed) to obtain the Third word.
  • Again, press the * key when the last digit of the timer is equal to the number of Stenography modules on the bomb.
  • Input the First, Second, and Third words’ strokes (in that exact order), pressing the “RELEASE” button after inputting each stroke.
    • Once you input the First word’s stroke and press the “RELEASE” button, you’ll have 10 seconds to input the other two words’ strokes until the module strikes.
