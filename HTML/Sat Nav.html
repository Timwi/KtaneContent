On the Subject of Sat Nav

The most useless Sat Nav ever.

When the display is pressed, a car will follow a series of turns, then a compass will be revealed. For three rounds, select the direction the car is now facing.

In Round 1, there will be four turns. In Round 2, there will be six. In Round 3, there will be eight.

The starting direction in the first two rounds is always North, but in Round 3, it will be random.

Selecting an incorrect answer will cause a strike and reset the current round.
