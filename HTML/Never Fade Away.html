On the Subject of Never Fading Away

Wake the fuck up, defuser! We have a bomb to solve.

  • The three tarot cards will be corrupted for different amounts of time, at different frequencies.
  • Using the table below, each card can be associated with a boolean condition based on its value and the current stage. Press the only card with a true condition. Doing so will swap out the cards with new ones, and reconfigure the nature of the corruption.
  • Reversals (upside-down cards) invert their own boolean value.
  • After pressing three cards, take the positions of the pressed cards in order (treating positions as numbers where 1 is left and 3 is right). Each of the Major Arcana is also associated with a transformation. Apply the 3 transformations associated with the pressed cards in order, then use the resulting sequence of positions to tell the future of the lowly mercenary.
