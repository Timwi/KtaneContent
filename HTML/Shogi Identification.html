On the Subject of Shogi Identification

It's like chess but... how do I read these?

The module will feature a shogi piece with a kanji character on it. Pressing the piece will flip the module over to reveal a 5×5 grid of squares.

Identify the piece from the character on it. Imagine the piece as being placed in the middle of the 5×5 grid. Press all squares which it can capture. Pressing the center square is optional.
