Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Puppet

On the Subject of Puppet

“Ninety Years We’ve Been Suffering Tik Tok Tik Tok.”

On this module you will see the Prize Corner from Five Nights at Freddy’s 2.

Keep The Marionette in the box by holding the square button. If it becomes empty you will be jumpscared and the module will strike and reset to 15 seconds but you will get 15 seconds of the music box reactivating as a cooldown.

The Music Box will chime as long as it’s active and will always be active until 80% of the modules in the bomb are done.
