On the Subject of The Funny Number

I prefer 25 myself.

  • To disarm this module, you have to enter the number that, when added to the displayed number, will equal 69.
  • If the displayed number is greater than 69, use the negative “-” to subtract from the displayed number to get 69.
  • Once you have the correct input, you can press the submit button to disarm the module.
  • Inputting anything other than the correct answer will result in a strike.
  • If you make a mistake, you can press the cancel button to reset your input.
  • If the displayed number is equal to 69, you can press submit without inputting anything.
