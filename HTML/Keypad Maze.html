On the Subject of Keypad Maze

Isn’t it number pad maze? I don’t see random nonsense symbols here.

You’re in one of the 6×6 mazes shown below.

The display on the left shows a digit and adjacent walls. Hovering cursor over display’s edges will reveal arrows which can be pressed and will move you in the corresponding direction in one of the mazes on the following page. Attempting to cross a wall will do nothing. Certain tiles in the maze will cause the display to turn yellow.

You can move freely in the maze. Based on adjacent walls, figure out in which maze you are and what is your current position.

Every cell has two values: position number (shown in the manual) and input number (shown on the module’s display). Find the four yellow cells in your maze, take their values and place every input number in order given by position number to form the four-digit code to submit.

Submit the received code with the keypad to the right.
