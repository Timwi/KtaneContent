On the Subject of Literally Deaging

The Fountain of Youth has nothing against this birthday cake.

If there is not a selectable birthday cake, you are looking at a different Needy Module.

This needy module features an aging emoji and a birthday cake.

In order to deactivate the needy, press the birthday cake to celebrate the emoji’s birthday.

The emoji bends time and space, so there is a barrier around the emoji to prevent it from catastrophic disasters.

If you do not make the emoji eat the birthday cake, the emoji will remain elder with back problems and a strike will be given.
