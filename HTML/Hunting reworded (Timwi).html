This module must be defused in four stages by pressing valid pictogram buttons. In each stage, determine which pictograms are invalid as follows:

  • The pictograms depicted on the top of the module indicate a row and column of a cell in the grid below.
  • Find the current stage number in Table A or B depending on whether the column pictogram is on the left or right.
  • Within the cells indicated in this stage and all earlier stages, find the pictograms located at the positions found in Table A or B. These are the invalid pictograms.
  • Press any valid pictogram to advance to the next stage. Pressing an invalid pictogram will completely reset the module.
