On the Subject of Not Wireswords

Wires are the main features of KTaNE! Wait, no, Simon is the norm. Wires are more like the second or third. Morse? No matter...

A Not Wiresword module will always have 6 wires on it. Each wires represents a letter, and to disarm the module, you must cut each wires in a way in which the order of the wires that have been cut will spell a valid password. If two or more wires represent the same letter, always cut the one on top first.

On colourblind-friendly modules, the wires may have patterns that can be used to determine the colours.

Use the tables below to determine the letter for each wires.

If the serial number contains a vowel:
