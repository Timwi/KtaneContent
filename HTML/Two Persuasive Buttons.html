On the Subject of Two Persuasive Buttons

Click me! Hold on a minute! Nobody loves you more than me. No way, I love you the most. Who do you choose, defuser?

These two buttons will want you to perform some action. However, one button takes a higher priority above the other. Based on the button labels, determine which direction the arrow is pointing at. The direction where the arrow is pointing at is the button with higher priority. Select the correct buttons three times in a row to solve the module.
