On the Subject of Matching Signs

Oh, you’re a scorpio? I’m sorry, but I can’t be with you.

  • The module will show 12 horoscopes on tiles, all of which can be selected and deselected.
  • Each horoscope can be paired with another horoscope on the mod.
  • No horoscope can belong to more than one pair, nor can it be unpaired.
  • Find an arrangement such that there are six pairs of horoscopes.
  • The following chart will show what horoscopes can be paired. Any big horoscope can be paired with any small horoscope beside it.
