On the Subject of Small Talk

No time for chit chat.

Note: If any word or phrase from Step 1 of this module is not written on the text display, you are looking at the wrong manual.

A “Small Talk” module has a text display across the top of the module, with six buttons below it with various words. There are three lights to the right of the button panel. Enter three short statements to disarm the module.

The text on the display is present on one of the many tables below. On the same table are three short phrases. Input all words from the phrase with the most words present in order. Repeat this three times.
