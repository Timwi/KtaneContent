On the Subject of Withered Foxy

“This needy could’ve gotten Scott Cawthon on us…”

Sometimes Withered Foxy will be in the hallway with this sound:

Fnaf 2 Hallway Ambience

Use the ctrl key to use the flashlight to spam flash him in the hall 20 times to deactivate the needy. You will have 50 seconds (or 90 on Twitch Plays) to get rid of him. Failing to do so will cause a jumpscare which results in a strike. Every time he leaves you will hear the solve sounds for Double-Oh/Cursed Double-Oh. You will know when he’s on your bomb if you hear the phrase “Be sure your step, through the heart of madness.”
