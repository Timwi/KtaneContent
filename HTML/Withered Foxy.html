On the Subject of Needy Withered Foxy

“This needy could’ve gotten Scott Cawthon on us…”

Sometimes Withered Foxy will be in the hallway with this sound.

Fnaf 2 Hallway Ambience

Use the ctrl key (or whatever command on Twitch Plays) to use the flashlight and spam flash him in the hall 20 times to deactivate the needy. You will have 50 seconds (or 90 on Twitch Plays) to get rid of him. Unable to do so will cause a jumpscare which results in a strike Every time he leaves you should hear the solve sounds for Double Oh/Cursed Double Oh. You will know when he’s on your bomb if you hear… “Be Sure your step, Through the heart of madness.”
