On the Subject of Piano Keys

What do you get when you drop a piano down a mine shaft? A flat minor.

  • A piano keys module will present with 3 musical symbols in the top indicator and a 12-note keyboard.
  • Each rule consists of one or more required symbol(s) and potentially other requirements too.
  • Follow the table of rules from top to bottom until the conditions in one row are all met - then execute the sequence of notes listed.
  • A failed attempt will require re-entry of the entire note sequence.
