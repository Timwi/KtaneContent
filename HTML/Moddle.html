On the Subject of Moddle

Who needs words when you’ve got Hexamaze, Big Circle, Switches, Boozleage, ❖, Totally Accurate Minecraft Simulator, ...?

  • A module will be chosen from the Repository of Manual Pages. Enter this module name into Moddle to solve Moddle.
  • Enter modules into Moddle by typing their Periodic Table Symbol with your keyboard, and then pressing enter.
  • When a module is entered into Moddle, the five squares in the center will show clues.
  • The solution module as well as any modules entered into Moddle must:
    • Be a solvable module.
    • Have a periodic table symbol consisting of 4 or fewer alphanumeric characters.
    • Have a Twitch Plays score.
  • Entering a combination of letters which doesn’t exist as a periodic table symbol will cause a strike.
  • Failing to submit the solution module within 8 guesses will reset Moddle and regenerate the solution module.
  • Pressing the up and down arrows will cycle through previous queries.

Clue Information

  • The first square represents the module author.
    • Green: The submitted and solution modules both have the same primary author.
    • Yellow: The submitted and solution modules share at least one contributor.
    • Gray: The submitted and solution modules share no contributors.
    • The primary author is the author listed first in the author list.
    • “Contributors” refers to all types of contributors, including hidden ones (such as Maintainer or Modeling). Go to the Options tab and check “All contributors” to list all names.
  • The 2nd–5th squares represent the publishing date, defuser difficulty, expert difficulty, and Twitch Plays score respectively.
    • Green: The submitted and solution modules have the same value for the field
    • Up arrow: The solution module has a higher/later value in this field than the submitted module.
    • Down arrow: The solution module has a lower/earlier value in this field than the submitted module.
    • In the case of a module with solve-based Twitch Plays score, assume that there are 10 modules on the bomb.
