Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Cryptic Keypad

On the Subject of Cryptic Keypads

You would think whoever makes these bombs would have stopped making keypads...

This module presents a 2×2 array of keys, which may or may not be rotated by a multiple of 90 degrees, bearing letters of the English alphabet. To solve this module, you must press the keys a specified order. Pressing a key out of order will cause a strike and generate a new array of keys.

Finding the Order of the Keys

For each key, look at the table below (which is in two halves). Find its letter in the column corresponding to which cardinal direction it is pointing to and mark it. The keys should be pressed in reading order within the table.
