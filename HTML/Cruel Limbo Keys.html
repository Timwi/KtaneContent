Pressing the center key will show you the initial key inside of the hypercube of keys and 32 rotations. The list of rotations is zero-indexed and consists of infinitely repeated list of those 32 rotations. After the animation finishes, the module's background will list those 32 rotations.

Also, after the animation finishes, a number (called N) between 300 and 999 will appear. Submit the position of the initial key after Nth rotation. To convert between the positions and the colors, use this rule: red - X, green - Y, blue - Z, brightness - W.

This module never resets. If an incorrect key was pressed, the initial key’s position will be shown briefly.
