On the Subject of Needy Piano

This module puts a whole new light on the saying “taking notes”.

This module contains a piano, and a display.

When this needy activates, the display will show 3 music notes. To disarm the needy, these notes must be played on the piano. To find which keys refer to which notes, look at the diagram below.

The notes are in the treble clef.
