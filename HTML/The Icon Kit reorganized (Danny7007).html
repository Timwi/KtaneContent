On the Subject of The Icon Kit

You better pick the cat icon.

  • Press the face to begin the module. This will display your Geometry Dash statistics. Press the shard button to view how many of each shard you have.
  • Four icons will be missing and must be filled in.
  • For each stage, select the first icon in reading order within the table whose requirement is fulfilled.
