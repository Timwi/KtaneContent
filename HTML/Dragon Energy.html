    Swapping Conditions Scenario

    1. If there is more than 10 batteries on the bomb and the serial's last digit is a 5 or 7: green-purple secondary words are swapped with the excluded green words, the green-red secondary words are swapped with excluded red words, the red-cyan secondary words are swapped with the excluded cyan, and the purple-cyan secondary words are swapped with the excluded purple words.
    2. Otherwise, if the module "Morse War" or "Double Color" is present on the bomb and there are more port plates than battery holders: green-purple secondary words are swapped with the red-cyan secondary words, and the cyan-purple secondary words are swapped with the green-red secondary words.
    3. Otherwise, if there is a [lit SIG and lit FRK] or [three unlit indicators]: excluded cyan words are swapped with green-purple secondary words, excluded purple words are swapped with green-red secondary words, excluded green words are swapped with red-cyan secondary words, and excluded red words are swapped with cyan-purple secondary words.
    4. Otherwise, if there are more than 8 modules on the bomb: the green-purple-red tertiary word is swapped with the green-red-cyan tertiary word, the green-purple-cyan tertiary word is swapped with the red-cyan-purple tertiary word, the quaternary section words are swapped with the green-red secondary words, and the excluded purple are swapped with the excluded cyan.
    5. Otherwise, if there are two or more vowels on the serial number: the quaternary section words are swapped with the purple-cyan secondary words, excluded green words are swapped with excluded red words, and the cyan-red secondary words are swapped with the excluded cyan words.
    6. Otherwise, if there are 0 solved modules on the bomb: quaternary section words are swapped with green-purple secondary words, and "Wisdom" and "Love" are swapped.
    7. Otherwise: swap "Wood" and "Dream", swap "Courage" and "Hate", swap "Freedom" and "Honor", and "Female" and "Dragon". Then proceed to find the last digit of the serial number and follow the swapping conditions of the respected swapping condition scenario that matches the last digit of the serial number. If the number is a 0, 7, 8, or 9, you may ignore the second swap.

    Stage 1

  • If all three words are in the same primary section, submit a word from the same primary circle.
  • Otherwise, if two words are in the same secondary section, submit a word from any secondary section.
  • Otherwise, if no words share a primary, submit a word from a quaternary section.
  • Otherwise, submit a word from a tertiary section.

    • Stage 2

  • If the three words are dispersed in a quaternary section, tertiary section, and secondary section separately, submit a word from a tertiary section.
  • Otherwise, if all the words are in some secondary section, submit a word from any secondary section.
  • Otherwise, if exactly two of the words are at the excluded primary level, submit a word from the quaternary section.
  • Otherwise, submit a word from an excluded primary.

    • Stage 3

  • Depending on what digit of the ones-place in the timer when you hit submit for stage 1, swap according to the "Swapping Conditions Scenario" to your already swapped version. Then depending on what digit in the ones-place in the timer when you hit submit for stage 2, swap according to the "Swapping Conditions Scenario".
    Note: 7->1, 8->2, 9->3, 0->4.
  • If at least one word was swapped at least three times from the beginning, submit a tertiary word.
  • Otherwise submit an excluded primary word.

    • Stage 4

  • Submit Dragon if it is in the quaternary section.
  • Otherwise, submit a word that has never been swapped.
  • Otherwise, submit Wisdom.

    Instructions

  • Consider on page 1: "Submit Avoidance". Never submit an answer when the appropriate number is on the timer of the bomb in the ones-place. The correct information is on Table A, Table B, or Table C depending on the edgework of the bomb.
  • Consider on page 2: Table D. You will be presented with three words in Mandarin and you will need to translate the words in to English to solve this module. If you can correctly describe the symbols, you will be able to match the English word.
  • Consider on page 3: Venn Diagram. Familiarize yourself with the terminology under the venn diagram.
  • Consider on page 4: the venn diagram on page 3 will be swapped according to the rules of the ordered list. Start at rule 1 and stop once you have matching conditions and perform the appropriate swap.
  • Consider on page 5: Stages. Follow the instructions on stage 1 to determine the first word to submit. Once the correct answer is submitted, the words will reset and you will advance to the next stage. If a wrong response is submitted, the words will reset, the bottom right colored indicator will randomly change, and a strike will occur.
  • Never submit the following words if they have been swapped at least once: Energy, Beauty, Soul, Love, Wood, Anger, Freedom, Devotion, and Hate.
  • If two of the words in the quaternary section during phase 4 are "Dragon" and "Energy", submit "Dragon" and then submit "Energy" for that phase. This overrules any other instruction for phase 4.
