On the Subject of Toxic Crystals

The power of alchemy is amazing!

The module consists of seven Crystals full of toxins embedded into some psychedelic casing.
You need to remove specific Crystals to prevent an infection of the entire bomb with toxins!

Three Crystals will be shining: those are the Concentrated Crystals.

Every time another module gets solved, the Concentrated Crystals and the letter shown on the module casing will change, starting with a “T”.

This letter is the first letter of the solved module’s name, ignoring all spaces and leading “THE”.
Characters outside the English Alphabet are considered as “?”.

Once a correct Crystal has been pressed, further solves will be ignored and the letter shown on the module casing will stop being updated.

Get your three Keys using the first rule that applies:

  • If all digits in the Serial Number are prime, use “089”.
  • Otherwise, if the all Serial Number digits multiplied together is greater than 38, use “520”.
  • Otherwise, if there are more Unlit indicators than Lits, use “216”.
  • Otherwise, if at least one Parallel port is present, use “347”.
  • Otherwise, use “038”.

For example, “Q” will use Middle-Left intersection as the first one, Bottom-Right as the second, and Top-Center as the third.

Shift the first Intersection 2 cells down in the table on Page 1, do not shift the second Intersection, and shift the third Intersection 5 cells down (wrapping up if necessary).
If a shifted cell lands on another cell that is already marked, keep both at the same location.

Those are now your Final Crystals.

On the module, press all three Final Crystals in the same order you obtained them to remove them.

If you must press a Crystal that already has been removed, press the next available one following their numbered positions (wrapping if necessary).

Pressing the wrong Crystal or pressing a correct Crystal in the wrong order will result in a Strike.
Strikes do NOT change anything about the module.
Keep in mind that other solves will change both the Letter on the casing AND the Concentrated Crystals.

*Prime numbers that can appear in this module are 2, 3, 5 and 7.
**Note that Intersection positions such as “Top Left” are the visual position in the table in Page 1. This means that no matter if the Keys are 148 or 841, TopLeft will always be using Key 1 because it is visually above 4 and 8.
