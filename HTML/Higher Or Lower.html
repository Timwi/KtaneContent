On the Subject of Higher Or Lower

There will be a lot of ups and downs with this module.

  • For each press, use the row corresponding to the color of the number, and the column corresponding to the amount of consecutive correct button presses plus one from left to right.
  • If the number on the module at the current step of the sequence is higher than the one in the manual, press the upper half of the module. Otherwise, press the lower half.
  • Repeat until solved.
