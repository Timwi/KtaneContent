On the Subject of [BIG SHOT]

Least informative manual ever.

When unactivated, the module displays, well, whatever that →
is.

Your character is a heart. Use the WASD keys to move.

The module activates after a random amount of time. When it activates, you are taken to either a minigame, or a deal screen.

If you are taken to the deal screen, move over a prompt until it turns yellow, and press SPACE to choose the prompt.
Only accept deals if they do not require any money from you and does not disadvantage you. Accept the final deal to solve the module.
If a deal seems ambiguous (say, 'I have a deal for you'), accept it anyway.

If you are taken to a minigame, survive for an amount of time. There are 2 main obstacles:

  • Hearts: There are grey hearts spinning around the module. Do not touch them.
  • Weird flying shit: Avoid them. After a while, they'll despawn and a new one will spawn from a corner.

Note that multiple obstacles may appear simultaneously.
