Step 1: Determining the target pressure

  • Use this: (− button value)×(screen value)/(+ button value)
  • If less than 11, add 4. If more than 35, subtract 6. (11 ≤ X ≤ 35)
  • If decimal is .5 or lower, round down. Otherwise, round up.

Glossary:

  • ALL MONTHS AND DAYS are abbreviated
  • Even month: Even months are February, April, June, August, October and December
  • S/N: serial number
  • D: D Batteries
  • AA: AA batteries
  • Bat.: Battery count
