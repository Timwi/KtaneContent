On the Subject of Deaf Alley

All the names that made sense were taken, so here’s a module name that makes no sense.

The module appears blank, but when hovered over may make a tone. The sections of the module that make a tone will create a shape. Feel all around the module to figure out the shape, then tap the 4 regions of the module in the correct order to solve the module. If an incorrect region is pressed, a strike will be recorded and a new shape will make a tone.
