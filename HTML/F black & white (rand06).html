Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod F

On the Subject of F

The best fox murder drone

On the module there is a letter F. There are also two triangles in colors that start with F. The F will also be in a color starting with F. There can be duplicates. These triangles are used as fox ears. Press the F at the correct time to solve the module. Use the venn diagram below to find a character and the corresponding rule. The possible colors on the module are Feldspar, Fern, Firefly, Fizz, Flame, Flirt, Forest, Frost, Fuchsia, Funk.

There is an indicator that is FRK or FRQ. The left ear’s color is a warm color. The right ear’s color is a cold color. Color of the F contains the letter E. Serial Number contains at least one of the alphanumeric characters in FØX.
