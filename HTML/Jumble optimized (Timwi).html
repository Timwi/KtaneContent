On the Subject of Jumble

Five words walk into a module...

Obtain the Jumble Number as follows. Accompanied by a flashing border and audible alarm, the number is shown for 8 seconds on the last display at bomb initialization. Upon a strike, the number and alarm will flash and sound again.

Take the Jumble Number modulo 5 and add 1. Read the jumbled word on that display.

Find the corresponding unjumbled word in the table to find the predefined button sequence for that word.

Press the buttons in the order from the sequence. If the Jumble Number is above 60, input the buttons in reverse order.
