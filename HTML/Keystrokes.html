On the Subject of Keystrokes

They’re called that despite the fact that you don’t actually stroke the keys.

The keys needed to type an item from one of the nine categories below on a QWERTY keyboard, excluding the spacebar, will be shown in red. Pressing the spacebar will change the arrangement of red keys to display another such item. There are a total of five unique items displayed on the module.

Three of the displayed items will be from one category, and one of them will be from another. The fifth item is from a third category, but 1 to 5 of its keys will be the opposite color. Press those keys to solve the module.

  • If the two other categories are in the same row or column, the solution display’s category is in that row or column.
  • If the two other categories are in the different rows or columns, the solution display’s category is in the third row or column.

Pressing a non-spacebar key while the keyboard is displaying a non-solution display or pressing an incorrect key will cause a strike. Under no circumstances will the module reset or discard correct input.
