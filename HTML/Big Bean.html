On the Subject of Big Bean

Me and the boys at 3 am praising the B E A N

This module has a big bean on it. This bean is either not shaking or shaking. Additionally, each bean can be tinted slightly yellow, orange or green. To solve the module, you must consume the bean by taking three bites (but no more). Eating the bean incorrectly will cause a strike. Note that you cannot see a bean when it has been eaten.

Eating The Big Bean

To find out how to eat the bean, plug all of the bean's information into the table below. This will tell you how to eat it. Each number corresponds to how many times a bite must be taken within a second starting from the first press for that number.

Wobbly beanNot wobbly bean
Orange bean 2, 11, 2
Yellow bean 31, 1, 1
Green bean 1, 22, 1
