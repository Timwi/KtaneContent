On the Subject of The Troll

Why is the box coller on this module so enormous?

  • To prep, add the following, and press that many times:
    • Solvable modules that are not The Troll
    • Solved modules that are not The Troll
    • 1
  • Solve any two modules.
  • To disarm, press when last digit of seconds is equal to battery count.
  • Further Notes:
    • Prepping with too many presses will strike (and reset).
    • Pressing without solving two modules will strike (and reset).
    • Solving more than two modules will strike (and reset).
    • Pressing at the wrong time will strike (and reset).
    • Not pressing enough times will not strike, but will return to dormant state.
