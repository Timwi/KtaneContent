On the Subject of The Burnt

Did you know: This module connects to the internet.

The flames change constantly. Click on the flames once to prevent them from changing.

If a voltage meter is present:

  • If voltage > #red: subtract 2 from the exponent.
  • If #green ≥ 8: Submit when the timer’s last digit = (#red - #blue).

Fan the flames a second time when the last digit of the seconds timer equals the digital root of (#red-#blue)10-#green.
