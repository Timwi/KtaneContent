On the Subject of A Rectangle

It’s not a square.

Note: A SquareA Square is a rectangle.

There is a rectangle.

Highlighting a rectangle makes a rectangle change color.

Highlighting a rectangle at a certain time makes a rectangle change to any of these colors:

Orange Pink Cyan Yellow Lavender
Brown Tan Blue Jade Indigo

Colors in this table are numbered from 0-9 in reading order.

The color of a rectangle will change if you focus on a rectangle when the last digit of the timer is different.

Usually, the color’s position in the table does not match up to the timer digit. Only one will match up.

Tap a rectangle, but do not hold it over a timer tick, when its color could match the timer in order to solve a Rectangle. A rectangle will turn green upon solving. If any input is wrong, a rectangle will temporarily turn red and a strike will be issued.
