Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Z

On the Subject of Z

Triplets, now?

This module initially is covered by a rectangular panel. When highlighted, this module will show the letter Z. If you see an X or Y, you are looking at a different module.

The Z key on your keyboard is linked to this module. Pressing the key will open the panel. The panel always rotates open.

Once opened, the squares flash a sequence of black and white, corresponding to a specific word in the list below. However, the Z has been removed from the word, and there is no gap between the end and start of it.

Treating exactly one square being white as a signal, the squares will transmit Morse Code.

To submit an answer, select at the module and hold down the Z key. Music will begin playing with strong clicks on the beat. Release after exactly as many beats have passed as the position of the word in the list. Releasing after 0 or 8 beats will have no penalty, however a wrong answer will incur a strike.

  2. Zealous
  3. Zipping
  4. Zodiacs

  6. Zoology
