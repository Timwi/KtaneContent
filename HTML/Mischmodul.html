Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Mischmodul

On the Subject of Mischmodul

The Germans were particularly fond of jigsaw puzzles.

The module will display an icon of a module. The icon has been segmented into 25 square pieces and has been randomly ordered. To solve the module, reorder the pieces so that the icon is reassembled. Select two pieces in order to swap their locations.

Pressing the button on the top of the module will cause all tiles in incorrect positions to glitch, flickering shortly.
