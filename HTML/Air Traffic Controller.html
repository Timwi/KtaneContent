On the Subject of Air Traffic Controller

Someone must be awfully upset with delays during your shift, maybe that's why there's a bomb on your desk.

  • Indicators on the left side are origins, while indicators the right side are destinations.
  • Check the departure and arrivals list in the correct origin table for the destination with matching colors.
  • If the destination is an arrival, angle the matching plane downward.
  • If the destination is a departure, angle the matching plane upward.
  • If the destination is not on either list, the matching plane should be level at cruising altitude.
