On the Subject of Run-On Sentences

And it goes on and on and on and on and on and on and on and on and on and on and on and on and on and on and on and on

The ticker tape contains scrolling text of a run-on sentence describing one of the cells in the table below with a red button and a green button above and twelve other buttons below which, when pressed, change the properties of the shape on the screen at the top of the module.

Pressing the red button at the top left of the module resets the ticker tape to the start of the sentence and allows the defuser to reread the scrolling text of the run-on sentence generated by the module.

Using the buttons at the bottom, change the shape at the top so that its pattern, shape, and background color match the cell being described by the scrolling text on the ticker tape and then press the green button which, if correct, will disarm the module.
