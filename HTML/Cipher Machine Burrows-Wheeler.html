On the Subject of the Burrows-Wheeler CM

A machine that knows no bounds when it comes to ciphers.

  1. Start with a list of N empty lines, where N is the length of the encrypted word.
  2. Prepend the list with the letters of the encrypted word vertically (one letter per row).
  3. Sort the rows alphabetically.
  4. Repeat steps 2–3 a total of N times.

Let K be the number shown on the top screen. The Kth row is your decrypted word.
