On the Subject of Lengthy Lockdown

"I can’t wait for 2 weeks off of school!"

This is module in which you approve or deny certain numbers provided on a display based upon whether they are even or odd. This is a staged module, and messing up a stage will cause the entire module to restart.

Display

On the module, there is a number display. This display shows the current stage’s number.

Stages

On generation of the bomb (and/or any restarts due to a strike), the module will generate the amount of stages needed to be completed. This will be from 5-20. You must complete all the stages to solve the bomb.

Accepting

If the number in the display is even, you must accept it.

Denying

If the number in the display is odd, you must deny it.

Button Labels

When the defuser progress a stage, they will notice that the button labels will shuffle and change wording. Do not pay attention to this, as the buttons will function the same regardless of their wording.
