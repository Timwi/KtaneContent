On the Subject of Cursed

Dammit Edmund.

Uh oh! Looks like your bomb has been cursed! Use the table below to figure out what the curse does. This module can be solved at any time by pressing it, but the curse will stay until the end of the bomb. Curses cannot appear more than once on the same bomb.

Table of Curses

Curse of the Blind All modules (excluding Cursed) are replaced with a question mark and are not revealed until they are highlighted.
Curse of Darkness The room is much darker.
Curse of the Unknown The strike count and total strike count are obscured.
Curse of the Maze Occasionally two random modules on the bomb will swap positions. Note that this curse cannot appear if certain modules are present.
