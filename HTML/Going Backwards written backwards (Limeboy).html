Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Going Backwards

On the Subject of Going Backwards and Backwards again

!tsaf og attoG

  • To deactivate the module you have to enter the correct sequence of numbers.
  • Numbers will show up on the screen when you press it. Afterwards, the module will start counting down from 32 seconds. You must return the numbers shown backwards using the numbered buttons. The module won't keep track of what you've inputted. Press any button after you've inputted 10 digits to submit.
  • A sequence will always be 10 digits long.
  • A strike will occur when:
    1. The timer has run out.
    2. Submitting an incorrect input.
  • And this will reset the module.
