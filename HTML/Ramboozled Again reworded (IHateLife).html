On the Subject of Ramboozled Again

Not a pseudo-needy... yet.

Decrypt the display and buttons using the decryption table.

Caesar shift the display A steps backwards then move the letters B steps right to find a word on the word list.

Button is valid if sum of alphabetic position of its 2 letters %26 equal (A*B)%26. Press a valid button to proceed. Must press button for more than 1/2 of a second.

Repeat 4 times to solve the module. Display will change after a press, but not the button labels/colors.

Press the button when the seconds digit correspond to its color:

  • Black: Press when seconds digit = unlit.
  • Tan: Press when seconds digit = D batt.
  • Brown: Press when seconds digit = AA batt.
  • White: Press when seconds digit = lit.

After 4th press LEDs will light up:

  • Red = Wrong button.
  • Yellow = Right button, wrong time.
  • Green = Right button, right time.

Module reset on a strike.
