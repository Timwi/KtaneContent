On the Subject of Winning Line

Who in their right mind loses a game of Noughts and Crosses?!

When the display is pressed, triplets of X’s and O’s will briefly flash in the Noughts and Crosses grid, then four answers will be presented. For three rounds, select the answer with the winning line of the grid you just saw.

In Round 1, the grid will be revealed in rows. In Round 2, it will be revealed in columns. In Round 3, it will be revealed at random.

Selecting an incorrect answer will cause a strike and reset the current round.
