Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Tachycardia

On the Subject of Tachycardia

You should probably call 911 when you're done with this.

Periodically, this module will play a sound cue and send an object from the top right of the module to the bottom left. Press the heart while the object is over the bright part of the background to avoid striking. Pressing the heart at any other time will strike. To solve the module, hold the heart for at least 5 seconds. Note that this must be done when already pressing the module to avoid a strike. When solved, another strike on the bomb will incur two strikes from each solved Tachycardia. Time your solves wisely. Also note that the frequency of interactions increases the less time is remaining on the bomb.
