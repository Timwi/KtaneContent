On the Subject of Flower Patches

A whole garden? On a bomb? I don't be-leaf it!

There are 15 flowers displayed which can be 9 different colors: Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet, white, black, and rainbow. Use the Venn diagram and the table provided to determine which flowers to select. If an incorrect flower is selected, a strike occurs and the module will reset the flowers.

Notes:

Blue is the lighter shade of blue, and Indigo is the darker shade.

The primary colors are red, yellow, and blue. The secondary colors are orange, green, and violet.

The warm colors are red, orange, and yellow. The cold colors are green, blue, indigo, and violet. The neutral colors are white and black.

Prime numbers present on the module are 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13.

Composite numbers present on the module are 4, 6, 8, 9, 10, 12, 14, 15.

Positions are read in reading order. Colums are numbered from left to right, rows are numbered from top to bottom.
