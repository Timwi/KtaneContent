Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod The Forget of Many Things

On the Subject of The Forget of Many Things

Yo dawg, I heard you like modules...

This module presents ten numbered buttons with an LED on each, a stage indicator to the right of these buttons and a display at the bottom. The module will first display a stage before any non-ignored modules on the bomb are solved. Afterwards, it will display new stages after a module is solved.

When all non-ignored modules have been solved, the module will enter its input phase. To solve the module, you must press the digits of each of the displayed stages, in order. Pressing an incorrect digit in this mode will cause a strike and show what the correct digit would have been.

Basic Mechanics

For every stage that the module displays, the stage counter will show what stage it is displaying. The bottom display will show the name of a module and sometimes also give information for that module. The button LEDs will also sometimes light up to display information.

Some modules cannot display their information fully on the display. Their information is split into separate pages, which can be accessed by pressing the 4 and 6 buttons (acting as left and right respectively). The stage indicator will show small arrows on its bottom corners to show if there are multiple pages present.

If any non-ignored modules are solved before the module activates, the missed stages will be appended as pages onto stage 1.

Keyboard Support

This module has keyboard support, meaning that you can use your keyboard to press the buttons. You can press the buttons by typing that button's number on your keyboard, using either the top row of the keyboard or the keypad. Additionally, you can use the A and D keys to press buttons 4 and 6 respectively.

Forget It Not

Pages: 1 page

One of the button LEDs will light up. The answer for this stage is the button that lit up.

Forget Me Not

Pages: 1 page

One of the button LEDs will light up, giving the current number. In addition, the display will show which number came before this one. Follow the rules below to find the answer:

  • If either of the previous two numbers were 0, the answer is the largest digit in the serial.
  • Otherwise, if both of the previous two numbers were even, the answer is the smallest odd digit in the serial, or 9 if no such digit exists.
  • Otherwise, the answer is the most significant digit of the sum of the previous two numbers.
