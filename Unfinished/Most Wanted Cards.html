On the Subject of Most Wanted Cards

Of all the US military equipment to end up on a bomb, a deck of playing cards is somehow the most fitting.

See the below page for deck reference.

The module has eight playing cards from the US Army Iraqi Most Wanted deck, which can be cyled using the arrow buttons. Each card has a rank and suit, as well as a name, title and picture. Exactly two peices of information on each card will be incorrect.

Observe the starting card and determine which pieces of information on it are incorrect, as well as the actual rank and suit.

Use the incorrect information to obtain the indexes of two key cards, and use the rank modulo 8 to obtain the third, taking Ace as 1, and face cards as 11-13. If this is 0, use 8. If it is already a key card, add 1 until it is not.

Use the suit to obtain a piece of information. For each key card in module order from most to least significant, take a 1 if that piece of info is correct and a 0 if not. Convert the resulting binary to decimal, again taking 0 as 8, to obatin the card you need to sumbit.

Take the displayed rank of the card, handling non-numeric as above, and use it to index into the name associated with the picture on the card. If the picutre is unknown, use the card's actual name. Press the card when the last seconds digit is the alphabetic position of that letter modulo 10.
