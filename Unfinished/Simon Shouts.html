On the Subject of Simon Shouts

Try to wrap your head around this one.

Try to wrap your head around this one.

  • The module contains a 2×2 arrangement of buttons that may be red, green, blue, or yellow. Rotate this arrangement clockwise by an angle specified in Table A corresponding to the number of ports modulo 4.
  • Each button also flashes a code that translates into another arrangement of colors according to Table B. Rotate this arrangement similarly, but using the number of batteries modulo 4.
  • Find both arrangements in the following diagram. The buttons show your current position, while the flashes indicate your goal position.
  • Pressing a button will move you some amount of spaces in its corresponding direction. The module is solved when the goal position is reached.
  • The diagram wraps around in both dimensions (it’s a De Bruijn torus).
