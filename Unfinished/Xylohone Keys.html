Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Xylophone Keys

On the Subject of Xylophone Keys

What do I do when the rest of the orchestra is tuning up? Blow my drumstick?

This module contains a xylophone, with keys corresponding to the notes C, D, E, F, G, A, & B in that order. Each key may be one of 9 colors. There is also a play button above the xylophone, which when pressed, will cause the xylophone to play a song. When a note is being played, that key will also glow.

Obtain a number that corresponds to each key, using the table on page 2. Add the digit that corresponds to the received song to each digit in this number, and % 10 the result. Play the first note of the song that corresponds to the first digit, then the second note of the song that corresponds to the second digit, etc.

Digit: Song: Notes:
0 Take Me Home, Country Roads C D E, E C D, E D C
1 Twinkle Twinkle Little Star C C G G A A G
2 Up Theme F A F E, F A F D
3 Avengers Theme D, D D, A G, F E D
4 Old Town Road D D D A G F D D A G F
5 Gravity Falls Theme D, E F, A G A C
6 Ode To Joy E E F G G F E D
7 Old MacDonald C C C G A A G
8 Mary Had a Little Lamb E D C D E E E
9 Oh Susanna C D E G G, A G E C

A comma in the notes indicates a rest.

If you strike on the module, your inputs will be reset. If a strike occurs from another module in the middle of submission, or if another module is solved when it is relevant, your input will not reset, but the answer will change.
