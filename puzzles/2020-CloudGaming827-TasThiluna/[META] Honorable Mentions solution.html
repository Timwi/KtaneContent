Solution

The rhyming flavor text and title hint at using the feeder answers that are modules in Kudosudoku. The flavor text also hints at using the title.

The numbers by the first set of boxes are indexes into one of the two words into the title: HONORABLE (9 letters) or MENTIONS (8 letters).

The feeder answers can be sorted alphabetically and then applied to the letters extracted from the indexes to fill the boxes.
