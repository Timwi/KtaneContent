Solution

The puzzle follows the rules of Rotary Phone. The numbers received from this are: 746, 487, 348, 364, 348, 384, 733, 646, 334, 448, 847, 333, 687, 749, 896. These numbers can be translated to letters using the Multi-tap Phone Cipher. The dots represent how many times to tap a number.

This says: RING UP FIVE OH FIVE THREE NINE EIGHT THREE FOUR SIX TWO. The phone number isn't up anymore, but puzzlers who called received this audio:
