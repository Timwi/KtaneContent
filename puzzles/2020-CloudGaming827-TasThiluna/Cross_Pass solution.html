Now that the crossword is done, we need to extract an answer. As hinted by the title, every clue contains a word from Password (Cross-word/Pass-word). In addition to this, there are as many words in each clue as letters in their answer (for example, “Give full house, e.g.” is 4 words long and has a 4 letter answer). Finally, the Password words used are only the first 15 words in the Password table.

Take the letter in the same position as the Password word, then order the letters with the Password words to get the answer
