Reversed Pairings

Solution

The puzzle features edgework and a series of number pairs in blue and yellow. The colors hint at the Greek Calculus module. Based on the provided edgework, we can determine the values of all the Greek letter variables and find that four of them match the values we need:

  • μ = 7
  • κ = 8
  • δ = 9
  • β = 10

Next, the flavor text is a riff on the flavor text of The Jewel Vault. Replacing the numbers in the puzzle with their corresponding Greek letters gives a sequence of pairs, which can be converted into numbers using the tables in The Jewel Vault.
