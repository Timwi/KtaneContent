Heroes & Clipart

Solution

The puzzle presents six rows of two images linked with an arithmetic operation. The structure of the title (“X & Y”) hints at the Sonic & Knuckles module. Each image references the name of an object (hero, badnik, or monitor) from that module.

By finding the Base Code value for each identified object in the Sonic & Knuckles manual and applying the arithmetic operator, we obtain a series of numbers which, when converted to letters, reveal the answer.

Image 1 Image 2 Calculation Letter
Image: Spirit Airlines logo Squeak (from Garfield) 16 + 3 = 19 S
Object: Ghost = 16 Technosqueak = 3
Image: Burning wall Green shoes 13 + 7 = 20 T
Object: Fire Shield = 13 Running Boots = 7
Image: Butter Android 6 ÷ 6 = 1 A
Object: Butterdroid = 6 Butterdroid = 6
Image: Spike (Super Mario) Glitter 8 × 2 = 16 P
Object: Spike Bonker = 8 Invincibility = 2
Image: Bonkers (from Kirby) Speedometer 8 + 4 = 12 L
Object: Spike Bonker = 8 Miles Prower = 4
Image: Hen 1-Up mushroom (Mario Bros.) 14 − 9 = 5 E
Object: Cluckoid = 14 Knuckles Extra Life = 9

The resulting letters spell the answer.
