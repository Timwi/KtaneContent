Extraction Mania

Solution

The puzzle presents eight sets of KTANE modules and a bank of extraction methods. The extraction methods must be matched with the module sets in such a way that they yield the name of a step in a colored cipher module.

The flavor text “index them into their origins like 1, 2, 3!” indicates that we should take the letter (from the cipher’s color) at the position corresponding to the step number of that encryption method.
