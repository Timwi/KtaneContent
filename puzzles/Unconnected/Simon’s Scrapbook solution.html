Simon’s Scrapbook

Solution

In accordance with established Simon module tradition, the pictures hint at verbs beginning with “S”. The parentheses show the lengths of words. In each picture, one of the colors in Simon’s head flashes. The flavour text hints that these colors are to be translated into numbers using the rules of Resistors. Indexing into the verbs using these numbers spells out the solution.
